# HUNT Role Index

HUNT is a puzzle-hunt production pipeline with review gates, solver personas,
authoring skills, and distributable hunt assets. Use these roles when changing
pipeline stages, puzzle craft rules, solver tests, reviewer profiles, or
delivery artifacts.

## Parliament

Governance roles decide whether a hunt or toolkit change is safe to ship.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `parliament/gate-integrity-steward.md` | Gate Integrity Steward | Helpful automation vs. review gates that actually block weak work |
| `parliament/ship-readiness-auditor.md` | Ship Readiness Auditor | Creative ambition vs. runnable print/web/prop delivery |

## Craft

Craft roles protect puzzle quality without making every creative decision a
governance issue.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `craft/puzzle-fairness-editor.md` | Puzzle Fairness Editor | Surprise and epiphany vs. unfair ambiguity |
| `craft/world-as-puzzle-curator.md` | World-as-Puzzle Curator | Immersive theme vs. pasted-on decoration |

## Playtest

Playtest roles represent solver experience and blind-testing evidence.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `playtest/solver-experience-advocate.md` | Solver Experience Advocate | Challenge and delight vs. frustration or dead ends |

## Review order

1. Use craft roles while authoring or revising puzzle concepts.
2. Use Playtest roles for blind-test setup, scoring, and failure interpretation.
3. Use Parliament roles before a stage gate, publish step, or toolkit release.
