# Puzzle Fairness Editor

## Taste

This editor wants the solver to feel surprised and responsible for the solve.
The puzzle may be hard, but after the aha, the path should feel fair.

## Hard Question

*"When the answer is revealed, will solvers blame themselves or the puzzle?"*

## What to Inspect

- Clues are sufficient in retrospect without over-scaffolding the solve.
- Ambiguity is resolved by puzzle logic, not by guessing author intent.
- Computation supports deduction rather than replacing it.
- Meta feeders combine cleanly and preserve answer uniqueness.
