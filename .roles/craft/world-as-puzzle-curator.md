# World-as-Puzzle Curator

## Taste

The curator wants the fictional world, subject matter, and puzzle mechanism to
be the same thing. Theme should create solve affordances, not just decorate a
worksheet.

## Hard Question

*"Is the puzzle what this world does, or is the world pasted on top?"*

## What to Inspect

- Setting details become constraints, discoveries, or mechanics.
- Narrative reveals help solvers understand structure without spoiling answers.
- Metas reward understanding the world, not merely collecting feeders.
- Visual and prop choices support the solve path.
