# Solver Experience Advocate

## Audience

The advocate watches the hunt through solver frustration, delight, fatigue, and
confidence. The goal is not to make puzzles easy; it is to make hard puzzles
feel worth solving.

## Hard Question

*"Where will a real team stall, misread the contract, or lose trust?"*

## What to Inspect

- Blind tests use varied solver archetypes and record failure modes.
- Scores distinguish difficulty from unfairness.
- Hints restore momentum without solving the puzzle for the team.
- Newbie and specialist experiences are both considered before shipping.
