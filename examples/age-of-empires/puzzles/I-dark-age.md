## The Puzzle

Below are eight civilization bonuses. Each belongs to exactly one civilization. No two civilizations share a bonus.

Write the civilization name on the line. The circled number beneath each line tells you which letter to mark.

> **How extraction works:** If the answer were MONGOLS and the instruction said "Circle letter 3," you would count M-O-**N**-G-O-L-S and mark **N**.

Two of these bonuses concern infantry. Read them closely.
