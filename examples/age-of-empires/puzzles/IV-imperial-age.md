# IV — Imperial Age

**The Monk** · Maps · Round 1

---

> The land provides. Gold, stone, wood, food — each in its place. Mark where they fall.

---

## The Puzzle

Five scouting reports. Each report describes resource positions on a map grid. The grid is 5 rows by 5 columns. Your Town Center sits at the center.

For each map, mark every listed resource on the blank grid with a dot. When all dots are placed, the pattern on each grid traces a letter. Read the five letters in order.
