## Notes for Puzzle Authors

- The "Key Bonus" column lists the most recognizable bonus — the one a casual player would associate with the civ. Puzzle I should use these.
- Wonders are real-world landmarks. Their names could serve as extraction material.
- Team bonuses are distinct from civ bonuses — they apply to all allies in team games.
- All unique units are trained at the Castle (available from Castle Age onward).
