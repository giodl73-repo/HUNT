## Trait Application

Designing this puzzle, the first perception was not a clue list but a picture: the tower upgrade diagram drawn as a horizontal arrow chain, Watch Tower feeding into Guard Tower feeding into Keep, each node a box on a screen. That spatial image became the puzzle's header, grounding every word-level clue in a structure solvers could see before reading a single line of text. The extraction mechanic вҖ” circled index numbers acting like crosshair reticles aimed at specific letters in each term вҖ” translates the "zoom in on a tile" gesture of the game's interface into a paper-puzzle action, so the act of solving physically mirrors the act of inspecting a unit card.
