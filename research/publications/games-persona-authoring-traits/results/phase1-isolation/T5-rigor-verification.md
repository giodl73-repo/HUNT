## Solution Key

**Step 1 — Identify each term:**

| Fragment | Answer | Verification |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| I | **TREBUCHET** | Imperial Age siege unit; 200W 200G; built at Castle; countered by Cavalry and Bombard Cannons (units.md: Trebuchet row) |
| II | **ONAGER** | Imperial Age upgrade from Mangonel; effective vs. grouped units and trees; upgrades further to Siege Onager which clears forest (units.md: Onager row; maps.md: Black Forest description) |
| III | **WATCH TOWER** | Feudal Age defensive tower; upgrade chain: Watch Tower → Guard Tower (100F 250S, Castle Age) → Keep (Imperial Age) (techs.md: University Dependency Chain) |
| IV | **ELITE SKIRMISHER** | Castle Age upgrade to Skirmisher; counters archers; cost 25F 35W (units.md: Elite Skirmisher row) |
| V | **REDEMPTION** | Monastery tech; 475G; enables Monks to convert buildings and siege weapons (techs.md: Monastery Technologies table) |

**Step 2 — Extract first letters:**

| Fragment | Answer | First Letter |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| I | TREBUCHET | **T** |
| II | ONAGER | **O** |
| III | WATCH TOWER | **W** |
| IV | ELITE SKIRMISHER | **E** |
| V | REDEMPTION | **R** |

**Step 3 — Read top to bottom:**

T · O · W · E · R = **TOWER**

**Why TOWER is fitting:** The puzzle's spine runs through the Watch Tower upgrade chain (Fragment III), and the University technologies that strengthen towers (Guard Tower, Keep, Heated Shot, Murder Holes) appear throughout the tech data. The answer is structurally embedded in the game's own defensive-building vocabulary.

---

## Trait Application

The Rigor/Verification trait drove the design toward clues where every factual detail is a verifiable data point — costs, upgrade prerequisites, and counter relationships are all drawn exactly from the world data files with no invented or approximated values, so a solver who double-checks any clue against a reference source will find the answer confirmed rather than contradicted. The trait also required a self-checking extraction: solvers who arrive at any letter other than T-O-W-E-R have made a factual identification error, making the puzzle's own answer serve as a verification gate.
