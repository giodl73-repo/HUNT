# Condition L-T5 — Artist minus Rigor/Verification

## Puzzle

**Line of Sight**

Each clue below leads to a term from *Age of Empires II*. Write each answer, then take the indicated letter. Read the letters in order.

| # | Clue | [N] | Letter |
|---|------|-----|--------|
| 1 | Your earliest defensive building that fires arrows automatically at approaching enemies — built the moment you advance from Dark Age | [3] | ___ |
| 2 | The building where villagers are trained; garrison them inside and it shoots back | [2] | ___ |
| 3 | Unlike the palisade version, this barrier requires the hardest resource to mine | [6] | ___ |
| 4 | A University technology that eliminates the dead zone at the foot of your castle walls | [5] | ___ |
| 5 | The middle evolution — above Watch, below Keep | [4] | ___ |

Read your five letters top to bottom.

## Solution Key

| # | Answer | Letters | Extracted |
|---|--------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | WATCH TOWER | **W**-A-**T**-C-H-T-O-W-E-R | letter 3 = **T** |
| 2 | TOWN CENTER | T-**O**-W-N-C-E-N-T-E-R | letter 2 = **O** |
| 3 | STONE WALL | S-T-O-N-E-**W**-A-L-L | letter 6 = **W** |
| 4 | MURDER HOLES | M-U-R-D-**E**-R-H-O-L-E-S | letter 5 = **E** |
| 5 | GUARD TOWER | G-U-A-**R**-D-T-O-W-E-R | letter 4 = **R** |

Answer: **TOWER**

The theme is defensive structures and technologies — every answer belongs to AoE2's fortification vocabulary. The answer word TOWER is extracted from the very buildings and mechanics that define a tower's existence in the game: the Watch Tower you first build, the Town Center you defend, the Stone Wall you erect around it, the Murder Holes that eliminate blind spots, and the Guard Tower that your Watch Tower becomes.

## Omitted Trait Effect

Without the rigor orientation, the design proceeded by feel rather than verification — the clues were written once and not cross-checked against the game's tech tree, leaving two unconfirmed assumptions: that Watch Tower requires no additional prerequisite building in Feudal Age (it may require a Blacksmith in some versions), and that "Stone Wall" is the exact in-game name rather than a generic descriptor the game labels differently. No errors actually slipped into the letter-index extraction, which was checked instinctively during construction, but the factual claims in the clue text (particularly the age-advancement trigger for Watch Tower) were asserted with confidence rather than confirmed.
