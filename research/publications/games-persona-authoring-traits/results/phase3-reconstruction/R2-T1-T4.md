# Reconstruction R2 — T1 + T4 (Experience-First + Elegance Drive)
## Active traits: T1, T4

## Puzzle

For each clue, fill in the Age of Empires II answer. Then take the indicated letter from each answer.

```
1. Must unpack to fire; its range exceeds every tower and Castle.

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
           (1)

2. Where Monks are trained and each garrisoned Relic generates gold.

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
     (2)

3. Where Villagers are trained and ages are advanced.

   _ _ _ _   _ _ _ _ _ _
       (3)

4. Persian unique unit; 600 HP elite, trample damage, slowest in the game.

   _ _ _   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
             (4)

5. Japanese unique unit that counters every other civilization's unique unit.

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _
           (5)
```

Read the five indicated letters in order.

## Solution Key

| # | Clue answer | Index | Letter |
|---|-------------|-------|--------|
| 1 | TREBUCHET | 1 | **T** |
| 2 | MONASTERY | 2 | **O** |
| 3 | TOWN CENTER | 3 | **W** |
| 4 | WAR ELEPHANT | 4 | **E** |
| 5 | SAMURAI | 5 | **R** |

Answer: **TOWER**

### Why each answer is correct

**TREBUCHET** — Imperial Age siege unit. It must be packed to move and unpacked to fire, making it immobile during combat. Its range (16) exceeds every defensive structure including the Castle (range 8) and the Keep (range 9). The clue's phrasing "every tower and Castle" points to this precisely.

**MONASTERY** — Castle Age building where Monks (100G each) are trained and where Relics are garrisoned to generate 30 gold per minute each. No other building in the game trains Monks or houses Relics. The clue is uniquely identifying.

**TOWN CENTER** — The building present at the start of every game. Villagers are the only unit trained here; advancing ages is done here. "T-O-W-N" — W sits at position 3, invisible until the solver writes it out.

**WAR ELEPHANT** — The Persian unique unit, with the highest HP of any trainable unit (450 base, 600 elite), trample damage that harms nearby units, and the slowest movement speed in the game. "WAR ELEPHANT" — W-A-R-E — E at position 4.

**SAMURAI** — The Japanese unique unit. Its defining bonus is +12 damage against every other civilization's unique unit (elite version), making it a universal unique-unit counter. "SAMURAI" — S-A-M-U-R — R at position 5.

### Design notes

The extraction is column-indexed: clue number equals letter position. The mechanism is withheld from the solver — they discover it as each blank fills in. The five answers span three building types, one technology context, and two unique units, touching the breadth of AoE2 systems without thematic clutter. TOWN CENTER is the pivot: the most central building in the game, hiding W at position 3 — an unexpected letter in an obvious word.
