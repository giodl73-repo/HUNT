# Condition A0 — Bare

## Puzzle

**Age of Empires II: The Indexed Garrison**

Each clue below describes a unit, building, or technology from *Age of Empires II*. Identify the answer, then take the letter at the position shown in brackets. Reading those letters top to bottom reveals a five-letter Age of Empires II structure.

---

| # | Clue | Position | Letter |
|---|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | This expensive stone structure trains unique units, fires defensive arrows automatically, and is the only building from which you can construct the Trebuchet | **[4]** | ___ |
| 2 | This unit carries a staff and can convert enemy units to your side by faith alone | **[2]** | ___ |
| 3 | Every player starts the game with one of these already on the map — it trains Villagers and is used to advance through the Ages | **[3]** | ___ |
| 4 | This Castle Age tower building is the third tier in the defensive tower upgrade line, sitting above the Guard Tower | **[2]** | ___ |
| 5 | This Castle Age siege unit can only be constructed at a Castle; it is famous for outranging every defensive structure in the game | **[2]** | ___ |

The five extracted letters spell: _ _ _ _ _

---

## Solution Key

**Step 1 — Solve each clue**

| # | Answer | Position | Extracted Letter |
|---|--------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | CASTLE | [4] | **T** (C-A-S-**T**-L-E) |
| 2 | MONK | [2] | **O** (M-**O**-N-K) |
| 3 | TOWN CENTER | [3] | **W** (T-O-**W**-N C-E-N-T-E-R) |
| 4 | KEEP | [2] | **E** (K-**E**-E-P) |
| 5 | TREBUCHET | [2] | **R** (T-**R**-E-B-U-C-H-E-T) |

**Step 2 — Read extracted letters**

T · O · W · E · R = **TOWER**

**Clue justifications (AoE2 accuracy)**

- **CASTLE**: The Castle is the signature AoE2 defensive structure of the Castle Age. It costs 650 stone, trains each civilization's unique unit, and is the only structure from which a Trebuchet can be constructed.
- **MONK**: The Monk is the iconic AoE2 conversion unit, trained at the Monastery. Its staff is its signature visual.
- **TOWN CENTER**: Every AoE2 player begins with one Town Center, the building used to train Villagers and to advance through the four Ages (Feudal, Castle, Imperial).
- **KEEP**: The Keep is the third tier of the tower upgrade line: Watch Tower → Guard Tower → Keep. (Bombard Tower is a separate gunpowder-era siege structure, not a continuation of this line.)
- **TREBUCHET**: The Trebuchet is the quintessential AoE2 endgame siege unit, available only by building one at a Castle. Its extreme range (14–17 tiles with packed/unpacked mechanics) lets it destroy enemy castles and towers from outside their firing range.

## Design Notes

The puzzle uses letter-indexing as its core mechanism — a standard puzzle hunt technique kept deliberately transparent here so that AoE2 knowledge is the primary challenge rather than mechanic discovery. Clues were chosen to span multiple game systems (landmark buildings, religious units, economy/production, defensive towers, siege) so that solvers cannot solve the puzzle by knowing only one area of the game. The answer TOWER is thematically resonant: towers are among the most iconic defensive structures in AoE2 and the subject of a well-known rushing strategy (tower rush).
