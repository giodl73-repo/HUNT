## Puzzle

**DAMAGED INSCRIPTIONS**

*Five stone tablets have been recovered from a ruined castle. Each tablet bears the name of something famous from the Age of Empires — but time and weather have worn away exactly one letter from each inscription, leaving a gap. Restore every name. The five missing letters, read top to bottom, spell what once stood at the heart of every castle.*

## Solution Key

**Step 1 — Restore each inscription:**

**Tablet I:** `_ R E B U C H E T`
The missing first letter is **T**.
Restored name: **TREBUCHET** — the Imperial Age siege engine, the game's most powerful anti-building weapon, unable to fire while mobile.

**Tablet II:** `L _ N G B O W M A N`
The missing second letter is **O**.
Restored name: **LONGBOWMAN** — the Britons' unique Castle Age unit, the longest-range archer in the game.

**Tablet III:** `_ O A D   R A I D E R`
The missing first letter is **W**.
Restored name: **WOAD RAIDER** — the Celts' unique infantry unit, the fastest foot soldier in the game at 1.03 tiles per second.

**Tablet IV:** `_ A G L E   W A R R I O R`
The missing first letter is **E**.
Restored name: **EAGLE WARRIOR** — the Mesoamerican civilizations' unique unit, a swift infantry that fills the cavalry role without horses.

**Tablet V:** `V I L L A G E _`
The missing final letter is **R**.
Restored name: **VILLAGER** — the economic backbone of every civilization, the first unit trained, and the one every other unit exists to protect.

---

**Step 2 — Read the missing letters in order:**

| Tablet | Restored Name  | Missing Letter |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| I      | TREBUCHET      | **T**          |
| II     | LONGBOWMAN     | **O**          |
| III    | WOAD RAIDER    | **W**          |
| IV     | EAGLE WARRIOR  | **E**          |
| V      | VILLAGER       | **R**          |

**Missing letters: T — O — W — E — R**

**Answer: TOWER**

---

**Step 3 — Thematic close:**

The five tablets name a siege engine, an archer, a raider, a warrior, and a worker — the full range of Age of Empires II. The five missing letters name the one structure that stands over all of them: the watchtower that guards the villager, commands the field, and gives the castle its skyline.
