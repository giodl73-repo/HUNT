## Puzzle

**Age of Empires II: Build Order**

Each clue below refers to something in *Age of Empires II*. Identify the answer, then take the letter at the numbered position. Write it in the blank. Read the blanks top to bottom to find the five-letter answer.

| # | Clue | Position | Your Letter |

| 2 | The unit trained at the Monastery that can convert enemy units | **[2]** | ___ |
| 3 | The building every player starts with, used to advance through the Ages | **[3]** | ___ |

**Step 2 — Read extracted letters top to bottom**

T · O · W · E · R = **TOWER**

**Clue notes**

- **CASTLE** [4 → T]: The Castle is the signature stone defensive structure of the Castle Age. It trains each civilization's Unique Unit (e.g., Paladins for Franks, Samurai for Japanese) and costs 650 stone.
- **MONK** [2 → O]: The Monk, trained at the Monastery, uses faith to convert enemy units and relics. Its staff is its iconic visual identifier.
- **TOWN CENTER** [3 → W]: Every player begins with one Town Center. It trains Villagers and is used to research the Age advances (Feudal Age, Castle Age, Imperial Age).
- **KEEP** [2 → E]: The tower upgrade line runs Watch Tower → Guard Tower → Keep. The Keep is the third and final tier of that line. (The Bombard Tower is a separate gunpowder structure.)
- **TREBUCHET** [2 → R]: The Trebuchet is built at a Castle during the Castle Age and is famous for its extreme range, allowing it to destroy enemy castles and towers from outside their return-fire range.
