# Condition L02 — Leave Out: Blame the Player

## Puzzle

Five items from Age of Empires II. Each is a civilization, unit, technology, or map.
Name each item. The initial letters spell the answer.

---

1. The Franks train this unit at the Castle.
   It throws its weapon.
   Stone walls do not stop it.

2. In the Imperial Age, siege engines can be ordered to clear trees.
   The machine that does this is an upgrade of the Mangonel.
   Name it.

3. The Celts field infantry faster than any other civilization can.
   Their unique unit holds that distinction to the extreme.
   It is the fastest infantry in the game.

4. This Feudal Age unit upgrades at no gold cost.
   The upgraded form hard-counters the unit class that threatens the answer to clue 3.
   Name the upgrade, not the base unit.

5. A monk walks up to a Battering Ram and stares at it.
   The Ram crosses to your side.
   Name the technology that made this possible.

---

## Solution Key

T: Throwing Axeman
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

Omitting "Blame the Player" allowed clue 4 to rely on a chain of unstated inference: the solver must identify the Woad Raider from clue 3, recall that infantry are countered by archers, recognize the Skirmisher line as the archer-counter, know that the Elite Skirmisher upgrade costs no gold, and then name the upgrade rather than the base unit — four reasoning steps with no fairness guarantee at any of them. Under the full principles set, clue 4 would require either a more direct path to Elite Skirmisher or a confirming fact that rules out competing interpretations.
