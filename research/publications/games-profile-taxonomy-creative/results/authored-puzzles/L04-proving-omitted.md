# Condition L04 — Leave Out: Solving = Proving Understanding

## Puzzle

Five items from Age of Empires II. Identify each one. Take the first letter of each name in order.

---

1. Built from a Castle, not a Siege Workshop. Costs 200 wood and 200 gold. Must unpack before it fires. Destroys buildings from a distance that towers cannot reach.

2. The Imperial Age upgrade to the Mangonel. The only siege weapon that can cut through forest — a critical property on Black Forest, where corridors are the only paths.

3. The Celtic unique unit. Speed 1.03 — the fastest infantry in the game. Trained at the Castle.

4. The Castle Age upgrade to the Feudal trash unit that hard-counters archers. Costs food and wood. No gold required.

5. A Monastery technology. Without it, your Monks cannot convert buildings or siege weapons. They can only convert living units.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption
Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

With "Solving = Proving Understanding" removed, a solver who knows no AoE2 can still reach TOWER by identifying each item through surface-level lookup — matching cost figures, unit names, and tech descriptions without internalizing why Monks cannot convert siege by default or why Woad Raiders are competitively significant. The clues remain factually accurate and mechanically grounded, but the answer no longer requires the solver to demonstrate that understanding; recognition of the names is sufficient.
