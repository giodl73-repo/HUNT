# Condition L09 — Leave Out: The Book Test

## Puzzle

Five items from Age of Empires II. Each description identifies exactly one unit, technology, civilization, or map. The first letter of each item, in order, spells the answer.

---

**1.**
The Franks train this unit at the Castle. It is classified as infantry, yet it attacks from range — it hurls its namesake weapon over walls and palisades. No other infantry in the game shares this property.

**2.**
The Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel. Its projectile does not stop at the first target — it passes through grouped formations and can fell trees, opening new paths through forested terrain.

**3.**
The Celts' unique infantry unit, trained at the Castle. It moves at speed 1.03. No other foot soldier in the game moves faster.

**4.**
The Castle Age upgrade of the Skirmisher. It is the designated hard-counter to the entire archer line. Group them on open ground, however, and a single volley from a siege weapon tears the formation apart.

**5.**
A Monastery technology costing 475 gold, with no prerequisite tech. Without it, Monks cannot convert enemy buildings or siege weapons — only living units.

---

## Solution Key

T: Throwing Axeman (Franks unique unit — ranged infantry, throws axes over walls)
O: Onager (Imperial siege — upgraded Mangonel, pierces formations, cuts trees)
W: Woad Raider (Celts unique unit — fastest infantry in the game at speed 1.03)
E: Elite Skirmisher (Castle Age Skirmisher upgrade — counters archers, weak to siege)
R: Redemption (Monastery tech — 475G, extends Monk conversion to buildings and siege)

Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

The Book Test is omitted because this puzzle is designed for solvers who can consult an AoE2 wiki or in-game Civilopedia. Clue 4 requires knowing the Skirmisher upgrade tier name, and clue 5 requires knowing the exact cost and scope of a Monastery technology — facts a casual player would look up rather than recall.
