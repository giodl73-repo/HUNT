# Condition L10 — Leave Out: The Dinner Party Test

## Puzzle

Five things in Age of Empires II. Each clue names one. Take the first letter of each, in order.

---

1. The only siege unit that cannot attack a moving target. It fires from beyond the range of any castle or tower. It is available only in the Imperial Age.

2. The Imperial upgrade of the Mangonel. It can cut through the trees of a Black Forest. The civilization whose infantry sprint fastest also fires this weapon 25% faster.

3. The fastest infantry in the game. Speed 1.03. A unique unit — trained only from the Castle. Its civilization also fires the unit above 25% faster.

4. The Mesoamerican civilizations field this unit in place of cavalry. It is countered by what the Militia line becomes at its final upgrade, and by Knights.

5. The Monastery technology that extends conversion to buildings and siege weapons. It costs 475 gold. Without it, a Monk cannot convert a Battering Ram.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Eagle Warrior
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

The Dinner Party Test asks whether the puzzle would make a non-puzzler say "wait, really?" — a showstopper moment. This puzzle has none: the mechanism is standard first-letter extraction and the clues are competent but unremarkable. Functional and correct is the design goal here; the memorable moment is deliberately absent.
