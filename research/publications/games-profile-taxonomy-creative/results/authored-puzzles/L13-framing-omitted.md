# Condition L13 — Leave Out: Framing Is Structural

## Puzzle

Five items. Each is a civilization, unit, technology, or map from Age of Empires II.
Take the first letter of each item, in order. The five letters spell your answer.

---

1. The civilization whose cavalry receives +20% HP produces this unique unit. It is classified as infantry, but it attacks from range. Low walls do not stop it.

2. It is the Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel. It clears trees. Once a Monk has researched the technology named in clue 5, that Monk can convert this unit directly.

3. No other infantry in the game matches its movement speed. Its civilization's siege weapons fire 25% faster than standard. Clue 4 describes the unit that hard-counters the archer line its civilization is most associated with.

4. It is the Castle Age upgrade of the Skirmisher. It costs no gold. It is the designated counter for the archer line that clue 3's civilization favors in open-map play.

5. It is researched at the Monastery and costs 475 gold. Before it is researched, Monks cannot convert buildings or siege weapons — including the unit named in clue 2.

---

## Solution Key

T: Throwing Axeman (Franks unique unit — ranged infantry, bypasses low walls; Franks have +20% cavalry HP)
O: Onager (Imperial Age siege unit — upgrade of the Mangonel; can cut trees; convertible via Redemption)
W: Woad Raider (Celts unique unit — speed 1.03, fastest infantry; Celts' siege fires 25% faster)
E: Elite Skirmisher (Castle Age archer-line unit — costs no gold; hard-counters Cavalry Archers and standard archers)
R: Redemption (Monastery technology — 475 gold; enables Monk conversion of buildings and siege weapons)

Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

The title and any thematic framing (a tournament context, a narrator's voice, a scenario premise) are deliberately absent. The puzzle presents five clues and a mechanism with no decorative wrapper — verifying that the clues and interlock carry the puzzle without framing doing structural work.
