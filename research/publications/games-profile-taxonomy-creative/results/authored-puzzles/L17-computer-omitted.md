# Condition L17 — Leave Out: Snyder's Computer Test

## Puzzle

Five items from Age of Empires II. Each is described by the clue below.
The first letter of each item, read top to bottom, spells the answer.

---

1. Imperial Age siege weapon trained from the Castle building itself. Outranges every defensive
   structure in the game. The only unit capable of destroying a Keep or Castle from outside its
   attack radius.

2. The upgrade to the Mangonel available in the Imperial Age. Can fell trees as well as units.
   A single shot clears a path through Black Forest walls.

3. The Celts unique unit. Trained at the Castle. Faster than any other infantry in the game —
   its speed bonus comes from the civilization itself, not from any technology upgrade.

4. The Castle Age upgrade to the Skirmisher line. Costs food and wood, no gold. Hard-counters
   the Crossbowman and every unit in the Archer line.

5. The Monastery technology that extends the Monk's conversion ability to buildings and siege
   weapons. Without it, a Monk cannot convert a Battering Ram.

---

Take the first letter of each identified item, in order.

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

Snyder's Computer Test is omitted. All five clues are resolvable by keyword match against the
world data tables — no inferential gap requires deduction, and a script with access to the
reference files could identify each item mechanically. The puzzle is valid as a vocabulary
test of AoE2 knowledge but does not satisfy the deduction layer the test demands.
