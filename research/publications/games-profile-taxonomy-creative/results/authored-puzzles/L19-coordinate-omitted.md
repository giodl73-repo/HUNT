# Condition L19 — Leave Out: Coordinate Answer Words Early

## Puzzle

Five entries below describe AoE2 units, technologies, or civilizations. Each entry identifies exactly one item. Take the first letter of each identified item, in order, to spell the answer.

---

**1.**
This siege unit unpacks to fire. Packed, it moves at a crawl. Unpacked, it cannot move at all. It deals full damage to any building. Cavalry is its natural end.

**2.**
This siege unit can uproot trees. It is the imperial upgrade of a unit that shreds grouped archers at Castle Age. The same unit that counters it in entry 1 counters it here too.

**3.**
This infantry unit belongs to a civilization whose infantry also move 15% faster than standard from Feudal Age onward. It moves faster than every other infantry unit in the game — speed 1.03. It is trained at the Castle.

**4.**
This unit costs no gold. It is a direct counter to the unit type that would shred entry 3 in open field. It becomes available at Castle Age but the base version is trained from Feudal Age. The upgrade removes its only notable weakness against the melee units it must avoid.

**5.**
This Monastery technology costs 475 gold and nothing else. Without it, Monks cannot target the units in entries 1 and 2. Researching it makes your Monks a threat to the heaviest and most expensive machines on the field.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption
Answer: TOWER

## Omission Notes

Because "Coordinate Answer Words Early" was omitted, the five items were discovered during clue-writing rather than pre-assigned. The items landed naturally: Trebuchet and Onager share a cavalry vulnerability (creating interlock between clues 1 and 2), Redemption connects back to both siege entries (interlock between clue 5 and clues 1–2), and the acrostic confirms the answer only after all five items are identified — no item could be changed without breaking the letter sequence.
