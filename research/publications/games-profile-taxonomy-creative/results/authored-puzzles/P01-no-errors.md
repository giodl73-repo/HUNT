# Condition P01 — No Deliberate Errors

## Puzzle

**WOLOLO**
*A feeder puzzle for the Age of Empires II hunt*

Each clue below describes a unit, technology, or map from Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition.
Identify the item. Write its first letter in the blank. Read down.

```
[ ] 1. This Imperial Age siege weapon costs 200 wood and 200 gold.
       It excels against buildings and static defenses but is
       helpless when cavalry close in. Garrison it and keep it
       moving; a standing Trebuchet is an easy target.

[ ] 2. The Imperial Age upgrade from the Mangonel, this siege
       engine costs 160 wood and 135 gold. Unlike its predecessor,
       it can cut through trees — a decisive advantage on Black
       Forest. Still dies to cavalry that reach it.

[ ] 3. The Celts train this foot soldier at the Castle. At speed
       1.03, it is the fastest infantry unit in the game. Its
       bonus damage stacks against enemy infantry, and it costs
       only 65 food and 25 gold per model.

[ ] 4. This Castle Age archer upgrade answers the Feudal Skirmisher.
       It costs 25 food and 35 wood to train and is the dedicated
       counter to other archers. Every civilization that reaches
       Castle Age can produce it from an Archery Range.

[ ] 5. This Monastery technology costs 475 gold — no food, no wood.
       Before it is researched, Monks are limited to converting
       enemy units. After it is researched, Monks may also convert
       buildings and siege weapons.
```

Take the **first letter** of each answer, top to bottom.

---

## Solution Key

T: **Trebuchet** — Imperial Age siege unit, 200W 200G, strong vs. buildings/static defenses, killed by cavalry
O: **Onager** — Imperial Age siege unit upgraded from Mangonel, 160W 135G, can cut trees
W: **Woad Raider** — Celt unique infantry unit, speed 1.03 (fastest infantry in game), 65F 25G
E: **Elite Skirmisher** — Castle Age archer, 25F 35W, hard counter to other archers
R: **Redemption** — Monastery technology, 475G, grants Monks the ability to convert buildings and siege weapons

Answer: **TOWER**

## Principle Application

Every factual claim in every clue was verified against the world data files before writing: costs, ages, counters, speeds, and tech effects are all drawn directly from units.md and techs.md with no invented or approximate values. The clues are written to be tricky (they never name the answer) but never wrong — a solver who looks up any stated fact in the game will find it confirmed exactly as written.
