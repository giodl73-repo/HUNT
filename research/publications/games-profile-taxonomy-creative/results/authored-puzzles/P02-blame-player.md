# Condition P02 — Blame the Player

## Puzzle

**WOLOLO**
*Five clues. Each describes a unit, technology, civilization, or map from Age of Empires II. Identify each item. The first letter of each answer, in order, spells the final answer.*

---

1. The only siege weapon in the game that cannot fire while moving — it must unpack before attacking and pack up to relocate.

2. The siege weapon that can destroy trees as well as enemy units; its predecessor, the Mangonel, cannot.

3. The unique infantry unit of the Celts — the fastest infantry in the entire game.

4. The unique unit line of the Mesoamerican civilizations, designed to counter cavalry despite being infantry.

5. The Monastery technology that allows Monks to convert buildings and siege weapons, not just enemy units.

---

**Final answer** (one word, five letters): ___________

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Eagle Warrior
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Every clue encodes a single, unambiguous property — a fact any AoE2 player has either read in a tooltip or learned the hard way in a game — so after the reveal the solver thinks "of course, that's literally what the tooltip says" rather than "how was I supposed to know that." The extraction mechanic (first letter) is the simplest possible, which shifts all the blame squarely onto the solver: if they knew the game, they had everything they needed.
