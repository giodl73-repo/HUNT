# Condition P03 — The Riven Standard

## Puzzle

Each clue below describes an Age of Empires II unit, technology, or civilization.
Identify each item. The first letter of each answer, read in order, spells your answer.

---

**1.**
This Imperial Age siege weapon cannot fire while moving. You must first issue the
"Pack" command, relocate it, then "Unpack" before it can attack. Its minimum attack
range means it cannot fire at targets directly beneath it.

**2.**
The Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel, this siege weapon deals area damage to
groups of units. It is the only combat unit in the game that can destroy trees,
allowing it to carve new corridors through the dense forests of Black Forest.

**3.**
The Celtic civilization's unique infantry unit, trained at the Castle. It moves at a
speed of 1.03 — faster than any other infantry unit in the game — even after the
Celts' own civilization bonus that already increases all infantry movement speed.

**4.**
This is a trash unit: it costs only food and wood, with no gold required. It is the
primary counter to archers, dealing bonus damage specifically against them and against
other units in the same class. It is the Castle Age upgrade of the base version.

**5.**
A Monastery technology that costs 475 gold and no other resource. Once researched, it
extends what Monks can convert beyond enemy soldiers — they may now convert enemy
buildings and siege weapons as well.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Every clue encodes a mechanical property — deployment state, tree-cutting, movement speed stat, resource cost structure, conversion target extension — that is only legible to a player who has operated these units or researched these technologies in actual play. The puzzle cannot be solved by pattern-matching on theme words or general medieval history; each answer requires recalling a specific game system that AoE2 uniquely implements.
