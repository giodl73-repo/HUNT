# Condition P04 — Solving = Proving Understanding

## Puzzle

Five clues. Each clue describes a specific item from Age of Empires II — a civilization, unit, technology, or map. Identify each item. The first letter of each answer, read in order, spells the five-letter solution.

---

**1.** This Imperial Age siege unit must be deployed before it can fire — while packed, it cannot attack. Its minimum range means enemies that rush inside the gap are completely safe from it. Nothing else in the game tears down a Castle from a distance quite like it.

**2.** An upgrade to a cheaper siege option, this Imperial Age unit does something no other military unit does: it can destroy trees, permanently altering the terrain. Its area-of-effect fire hits friendly units — a careless player can cut their own woodline or shred their own army.

**3.** This unique infantry unit belongs to the civilization whose infantry are already the fastest in the game — and this unit is faster still, clocking 1.03 movement speed, the highest of any infantry in the game. It is trained at the Castle.

**4.** Researched at the Barracks in the Castle Age, this upgrade transforms a Feudal-Age ranged unit into one of the best counter-units in the game. Its primary job is destroying other ranged units — it deals bonus damage specifically against the archer class it originally resembled.

**5.** This Monastery technology, available from the Castle Age, expands what the Monk can target. Before it is researched, Monks can only convert living units. After it is researched, Monks can also convert buildings and siege weapons. It costs only gold.

---

## Solution Key

T: **Trebuchet** — Imperial Age siege unit; must unpack to fire; minimum range; unmatched at destroying buildings at long range.

O: **Onager** — Imperial Age upgrade from the Mangonel; area-of-effect fire; uniquely destroys trees, changing the map permanently.

W: **Woad Raider** — Celtic unique infantry; speed 1.03, the fastest infantry in the game; trained at the Castle.

E: **Elite Skirmisher** — Castle Age Barracks upgrade from the Skirmisher; counter-unit specifically designed to destroy archers.

R: **Redemption** — Monastery technology; enables Monks to convert buildings and siege weapons; costs only gold.

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Every clue encodes a mechanic that is invisible without play experience: the Trebuchet's pack/unpack minimum range, the Onager's tree-cutting friendly fire, the Woad Raider's speed stat, the Elite Skirmisher's counter role, and Redemption's expansion of Monk targeting. A solver who reaches TOWER without knowing these mechanics cannot exist — the first letters are only recoverable by naming items, and naming items requires understanding what each description is pointing at.
