# Condition P05 — No Over-Scaffolding

## Puzzle

**WOLOLO**
*Five answers. First letters.*

1. The only siege weapon that must unpack before firing; even its name evokes the arm swinging skyward.

2. Its Imperial upgrade can tear through a forest — useful when the choke points have been walled and the trees are the only road left.

3. The Celts' signature infantry: not the hardest hit, not the toughest skin, just the fastest feet on the battlefield.

4. Archers fear it. Skirmishers fear it. It was born for exactly this: it specializes in killing the unit that specializes in killing archers.

5. A Monastery technology that crosses the line most Monks will not — turning an enemy's own fortress and siege engines against them.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption
Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

The puzzle states the extraction rule once ("First letters") and then presents five clues — no blanks to fill, no numbered answer lines labelled with letters, no instruction to "write the first letter of each answer below." If the five clues were stripped of the header, the solver who knows AoE2 would identify each item, notice the initials, and arrive at TOWER without any further guidance.
