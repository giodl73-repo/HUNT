# Condition P06 — Surprise the Answer

## Puzzle

Each clue below describes an Age of Empires II item — a civilization, unit, technology, or map. Identify the item, take its **first letter**, and read the letters in order to find your answer.

---

**1.** This Monastery technology lets your group of converting Monks share a single recovery period: only one of them needs to rest after a successful conversion, freeing the rest to act immediately.

**2.** This Imperial-Age siege engine is the only military unit in the game capable of destroying trees — cutting new corridors through Black Forest's walls of ancient oak.

**3.** This Celtic unique unit holds the record for fastest infantry in the game, sprinting at a speed of 1.03 — faster than any other foot soldier regardless of civilization.

**4.** This unit costs no gold at all — only food and wood — and exists specifically to hard-counter archer-line units, answering Crossbows and Arbalesters at a fraction of their price.

**5.** This Monastery technology extends your Monks' reach by 3 tiles, allowing them to attempt conversion from across a greater distance — a critical upgrade for any civilization running a pure Monk strategy.

---

_Take the first letter of each identified item, in order._

---

## Solution Key

T: Theocracy
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption (Block Printing was considered; Redemption chosen for thematic contrast with Theocracy — both Monastery techs, flanking the answer)

**Answer: TOWER**

---

## Principle Application

Every clue steers the solver into monk economy, siege utility, unit speed, trash-unit countering, and conversion range — domains that evoke the game's tactical depth rather than its defensive architecture. When the five first letters arrive as T-O-W-E-R, the solver has been thinking about faith mechanics, tree-cutting, and cost ratios, not fortifications, so TOWER lands as a genuine surprise: the word was hiding in plain sight inside a puzzle that felt like it was about everything except towers.
