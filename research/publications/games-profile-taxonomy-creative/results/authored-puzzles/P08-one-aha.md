# Condition P08 — One Aha, Not Zero, Not Three

## Puzzle

Five items from Age of Empires II are described below. Identify each one, then find what the five answers have in common.

---

1. This Imperial Age siege unit is the only thing in the game that can attack from beyond the range of a Castle. It cannot fire at targets within a minimum distance, and it must be packed and unpacked to move.

2. This siege unit costs only wood and gold, never food. In Imperial Age it is upgraded from the Mangonel. Its most unusual ability in the game is that it can destroy trees — and experienced players use this to cut new corridors through the Black Forest.

3. The fastest infantry unit in the game. Trained at the Castle, it belongs to only one civilization, whose civ bonus already makes all their infantry run faster. This unit moves at 1.03 speed — faster even than most cavalry.

4. The cheapest counter to archers, costing only food and wood — no gold. This unit upgrades from its Feudal Age predecessor in the Castle Age and is specifically designed to beat other ranged units at low cost in the late game.

5. A Monastery technology costing 475 gold. Before this research, Monks can only convert military units. After it, they can also convert siege weapons and buildings — enabling the aggressive tactic of flipping enemy Castles and Rams to your side.

---

*What five-letter word do the answers spell?*

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

The puzzle presents five factual clues that test genuine AoE2 knowledge — nothing in any clue hints at letters or spelling. The single aha arrives when the solver writes down all five answers and sees that their first letters spell TOWER; before that moment the mechanism is invisible, and after it the extraction requires no further insight.
