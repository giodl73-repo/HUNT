## Principle Application

Every clue embeds the identifying facts directly in its text — cost, age, source building, and key trait — so a solver who knows AoE2 can confirm the answer from the clue alone without consulting any external reference. A solver who does not know AoE2 cannot guess from the clue text alone, but that is the domain knowledge the hunt assumes; no wiki, database, or search engine is needed to verify or narrow any answer once the solver has basic familiarity with the game.
