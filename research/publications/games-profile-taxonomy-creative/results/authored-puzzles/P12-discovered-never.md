# Condition P12 — Discovered, Never Explained

## Puzzle

**Five answers. One word each.**

1. The only siege unit that can destroy a Castle from outside the Castle's own attack range. It cannot move while firing.

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

2. The Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel. Siege Workshop unit. Can target trees, making it the tool of choice for cutting corridors through Black Forest.

   _ _ _ _ _

3. The Celts' unique unit. The fastest infantry in the game. Speed 1.03 — faster than any cavalry archer at a sprint.

   _ _ _ _   _ _ _ _ _

4. The Castle Age upgrade of the Skirmisher line. Counter-archer trash unit. Costs no gold.

   _ _ _ _ _   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

5. Monastery technology that extends conversion to buildings and siege weapons. Without it, Monks can only convert living units.

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

The puzzle presents five numbered clues with blank answer lines and no instruction about what to do with the answers — the solver who writes in TREBUCHET, ONAGER, WOAD RAIDER, ELITE SKIRMISHER, REDEMPTION sees the first letters align into TOWER without being told to look. Explaining the mechanism upfront would transform a moment of discovery into a chore of confirmation, stripping out the only reward the puzzle has to give.
