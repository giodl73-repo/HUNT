# Condition P14 — Multiple Deduction Paths

## Puzzle

Each clue below describes a unit, technology, civilization, or map from Age of Empires II.
Identify the item named by each clue. The first letter of each answer, read top to bottom, spells a five-letter word.

---

**1.**
This siege weapon costs only wood and gold — no food.
It is built at the Castle building, not the Siege Workshop.
It is the only unit in the game that can unpack and fire while stationary, targeting buildings from outside their attack range.

**2.**
This siege unit is the Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel.
It can target and destroy trees, opening new paths through forested terrain.
It costs 160 wood and 135 gold, and deals area damage that makes it devastating against grouped units.

**3.**
This unique infantry unit belongs to the Celts civilization.
It is the fastest infantry unit in the game, moving at speed 1.03.
It costs 65 food and 25 gold and is trained at the Castle.

**4.**
This unit is the Castle Age upgrade of the Skirmisher.
It is the primary counter to archer-class units.
Its cost includes food and wood but no gold, making it a trash unit.

**5.**
This Monastery technology costs 475 gold and no other resource.
It extends the Monk's conversion ability to buildings and siege weapons, which Monks cannot target by default.
It is one of only two Monastery technologies that cost a resource other than gold.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Each clue supplies three independent facts — cost/resource type, build location or prerequisite, and battlefield role — so a solver who cannot recall one fact can triangulate the answer from the other two. No clue reduces to a single trivia point: every item is uniquely identified by any two of its three stated facts working together.
