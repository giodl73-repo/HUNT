# Condition P15 — No Computation Without Deduction

## Puzzle

Five questions about Age of Empires II. Each answer is a single AoE2 item — a civilization, unit, technology, or map. Take the first letter of each answer in order.

---

1. The only siege weapon that must stop moving and deploy before it can fire — and must pack up again before it can move.

2. The siege unit whose attack can fell trees, opening new paths through otherwise impassable forest.

3. The Celts' unique unit, and the fastest-moving infantry in the game.

4. The archer-class unit that is specifically designed to counter other archers.

5. The Monastery technology that extends a Monk's conversion ability to buildings and siege weapons.

---

What five-letter word do the initial letters spell?

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption
Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Every clue demands that the solver recall a distinctive AoE2 property — the Trebuchet's deploy mechanic, the Onager's tree-felling, the Woad Raider's speed record, the Elite Skirmisher's counter role, Redemption's expanded conversion target list — and identify the item that uniquely holds it; no numbers are given and no procedure can substitute for that recognition. Stripping out the five descriptions leaves nothing a solver can work with, confirming that the puzzle's only path is genuine insight into each item's identity.
