# Condition P18 — Verify the Last Mile

## Puzzle

Five things from Age of Empires II. Identify each one. Take the first letter of each answer in order. What word do they spell?

1. This Imperial Age siege weapon fires from outside castle range, destroying buildings without taking return fire. It packs up and unpacks to shoot — you cannot fire while moving.

2. This siege unit is the Imperial Age upgrade of the Mangonel. It still shreds grouped archers and packed infantry, but at this tier it can also target and destroy trees.

3. This Celtic unique unit is the fastest infantry in the game. Trained at the Castle, it closes the gap on archers before they can kite away.

4. This counter-archer unit upgrades from its Feudal form at the Castle Age. It throws a short javelin in return, making it the standard answer whenever your opponent masses archers.

5. This Monastery technology allows your Monks to convert enemy siege weapons and buildings — not just military units. It costs 475 gold and nothing else.

## Solution Key

T: **Trebuchet** → first letter: T ✓
O: **Onager** → first letter: O ✓
W: **Woad Raider** → first letter: W ✓
E: **Elite Skirmisher** → first letter: E ✓
R: **Redemption** → first letter: R ✓
Answer: T-O-W-E-R = TOWER ✓

## Principle Application

The mechanism (identifying the five AoE2 items) and the extraction (reading off first letters) are verified as two distinct operations: each item is confirmed to exist in the world data, and each first letter is traced character by character against the target word before the puzzle was written. The clues were written only after the T-O-W-E-R chain was proven letter by letter, ensuring the extraction cannot silently fail.
