# Condition P19 — Coordinate Answer Words Early

## Pre-Design Answer Key (built before any clues written)

T candidates considered: Trebuchet (siege unit), Throwing Axeman (Franks unique unit), Two-Handed Swordsman (infantry), Treadmill Crane (University tech), Theocracy (Monastery tech) → selected: **Trebuchet** (reason: the most iconic Imperial Age siege unit in AoE2, universally recognized, single clean word)

O candidates considered: Onager (siege unit — the only O-item in world data) → selected: **Onager** (reason: only viable candidate; fortunately it is a well-known and distinctive siege weapon, upgrade from Mangonel)

W candidates considered: War Elephant (Persians unique unit), Woad Raider (Celts unique unit) → selected: **Woad Raider** (reason: more distinctive cluing potential — its speed trait and Celtic origin are both highly specific, reducing ambiguity compared to War Elephant whose HP/trample traits are harder to pin precisely)

E candidates considered: Elite Skirmisher (archer line upgrade — the only E-item in world data) → selected: **Elite Skirmisher** (reason: only viable candidate; well-known unit, the upgrade that hard-counters archers, available Castle Age)

R candidates considered: Redemption (Monastery tech), Ring Archer Armor (Blacksmith tech) → selected: **Redemption** (reason: single evocative word with a clear and specific effect — Monks converting buildings and siege — making it far easier to clue cleanly than the compound "Ring Archer Armor")

## Puzzle

**WOLOLO HUNT — Feeder Puzzle**
*"Stone to Stone"*

*Five clues. Each answer is an Age of Empires II civilization, unit, or technology. Take the first letter of each answer, in order, to spell the final answer.*

---

**1.** This Imperial Age siege engine hurls boulders in a high arc and is the only unit that can attack from beyond a Castle's range. It cannot move and fire at the same time.

**2.** The upgraded form of the Mangonel, available in Imperial Age. It gains the ability to cut down trees — and will do so whether you intended it or not.

**3.** The fastest infantry unit in the game. Available to the civilization whose unique Wonder is the Rock of Cashel. Its speed is 1.03 — faster than any other foot soldier.

**4.** The Castle Age upgrade to the base Skirmisher line. It is the primary answer to a mass of crossbows, negating their damage with high pierce armor.

**5.** A Monastery technology costing 475 gold. Once researched, your Monks gain the ability to convert not just enemy soldiers but also enemy Siege Workshop units and buildings.

---

*Take the first letter of each answer. What five-letter word do they spell?*

---

## Solution Key

1. T: **Trebuchet** — "only unit that can attack from beyond a Castle's range," cannot move and fire simultaneously
2. O: **Onager** — Mangonel upgrade, Imperial Age, cuts trees
3. W: **Woad Raider** — fastest infantry (1.03 speed), Celts civ (Rock of Cashel wonder)
4. E: **Elite Skirmisher** — Castle Age Skirmisher upgrade, counters crossbows
5. R: **Redemption** — Monastery tech, 475 gold, allows Monks to convert buildings and siege

**Answer: TOWER**

---

## Principle Application

Before writing the first clue, every answer was identified and verified against world data — candidates were listed for each letter, the strongest option selected, and the full five-item key locked in. This front-loaded verification prevented the common failure mode of writing four polished clues only to discover no clean item exists for the fifth letter, which would require backtracking and rewriting previous clues to fit a new answer word.
