# Condition P20 — The Voice Rules

## Puzzle

Five AoE2 items are described below. Each description identifies one specific item — a civilization, unit, technology, or map. Take the first letter of each item in order. The letters spell your answer.

---

**1.**
You see a massive siege weapon in late Imperial play. It hurls stones at castles and walls from a safe distance. Cavalry must kill it before it levels your base.

**2.**
You upgrade this siege weapon from a Mangonel. It fires slower but hits harder. It can cut through trees and scatter groups of units with a single shot.

**3.**
You face this Celtic unique unit in open-field battles. It moves faster than any other infantry. It costs no gold and closes the distance before you can respond.

**4.**
You research this upgrade at the Archery Range in Castle Age. It counters opposing archers at close range. You send it ahead to absorb arrow fire for your knights.

**5.**
You research this technology at the Monastery. It allows your Monks to convert enemy siege weapons and buildings. Without it, your Monks cannot touch rams or towers.

---

Take the first letter of each item you identified, in order from 1 to 5.

---

## Solution Key

T: Trebuchet
O: Onager
W: Woad Raider
E: Elite Skirmisher
R: Redemption

Answer: TOWER

## Principle Application

Every sentence addresses the solver directly as "you" and stays in present tense, creating the sensation of a calm observer narrating a match as it unfolds. No sentence exceeds fifteen words, no question marks appear, and no sentence comments on the puzzle itself — only on the game.
