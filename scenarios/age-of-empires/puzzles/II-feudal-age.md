## The Puzzle

A single-elimination tournament. Eight units. Seven matches. Each matchup is decided by counter logic — the unit that counters the other advances.

One matchup breaks the rule you expect. A certain cavalry unit does not fear the spear.

Fill in the winner of each match. The number beside each winner's slot tells you which letter of that unit's name to circle.

## Counter Logic

The rules of engagement:

- **Siege** destroys archers and infantry. Cavalry destroys siege.
- **Cavalry** runs down archers and siege. Anti-cavalry infantry destroys cavalry.
- **Archers** shred infantry. Siege and cavalry destroy archers.
- **Infantry** falls to archers and siege. Anti-cavalry infantry is the exception — it exists to kill cavalry.

One unit in this bracket defies its counter. Its armor was designed to resist the very bonus that should destroy it.
