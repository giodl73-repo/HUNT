# Market Day

*The market is open. Resources are moving. Someone at the table is having a very productive turn. You're watching it happen — every trade, every build, every card drawn. Pay attention. When the dust settles, there's only one thing left to build.*

---

## Starting Hand

Your opponent begins the turn with the following resource cards:

| Resource | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Brick | 3 |
| Lumber | 2 |
| Ore | 1 |
| Grain | 2 |
| Wool | 4 |

**Total: 12 cards**

---

## The Turn

Five actions, in order. Track the resources.

---

**Action 1: Build a Road**

The player builds a road.

---

**Action 2: Trade at a Port**

The player trades Wool for Ore at a port. After this trade, the player has 2 Ore.

*Note: You must figure out which port the player used and how many Wool cards they spent.*

---

**Action 3: Build a Settlement**

The player builds a settlement.

---

**Action 4: Buy a Development Card**

The player purchases a development card from the supply.

---

**Action 5: Build Another Road**

The player builds a second road.

---

## After the Turn

After all five actions, the player has resource cards remaining. There is exactly one structure they could build with those remaining cards on their next turn.

**What is it?**

The name of the thing they can build — that is your answer. Five letters.

---
