## The Rules

1. Your clue must be **one English word**.
2. Your clue must relate to the **meaning** of the target words (not spelling, not position, not rhyming).
3. Your clue **cannot** be any word currently on the grid.
4. Your clue connects exactly **3** of your Blue words.
5. Your clue must **not** relate to BOMB (the assassin).
6. The connection must be **clear enough** that your operatives would pick the right 3 words.
