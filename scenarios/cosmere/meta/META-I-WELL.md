## Mechanism: The Pulling Metals Speak

### Setup

The solver has solved all 12 Scadrial feeder puzzles (P01-P12) and obtained 12 answer words. The meta page presents the 4x4 Allomancy table (the grid from allomancy.md) with 16 cells.

Each of the 12 feeder puzzles is identified with a specific Allomantic metal by its puzzle content:
- Module A puzzles (P01-P06) each directly reference a metal through their Allomancy-focused mechanisms
- Module B puzzles (P07-P12) each reference a metal through character associations (e.g., a puzzle about Breeze → Brass, a puzzle about Vin → all metals but use context to identify the specific one)

The solver places each answer word into the corresponding cell of the 4x4 table.

### The Extraction

**Step 1: Identify each answer's metal.**

Each puzzle's content maps to exactly one metal. The meta page provides the 4x4 grid with the 16 metal names pre-printed. The solver writes their 12 answer words next to the corresponding metals.

**Step 2: Apply the quadrant extraction rule.**

For each filled cell, the solver extracts one letter from the answer word based on the metal's quadrant:
- **Physical quadrant** metals → take letter **1** of the answer
- **Mental quadrant** metals → take letter **2** of the answer
- **Temporal quadrant** metals → take letter **3** of the answer
- **Enhancement quadrant** metals → take letter **4** of the answer

This yields 12 extracted letters.

**Step 3: Filter by Pull/Push.**

The 16 metals are divided into Pulling and Pushing metals:
- **Pulling metals** (attract, draw together): Iron, Tin, Zinc, Copper, Gold, Cadmium, Aluminum, Chromium
- **Pushing metals** (repel, separate): Steel, Pewter, Brass, Bronze, Electrum, Bendalloy, Duralumin, Nicrosil

Of the 12 filled cells, exactly **5 are Pulling metals**. The solver reads only the extracted letters from Pulling metal cells, in table order (ascending metal number).

**Step 4: Read the answer.**

The 5 Pulling-metal letters, in metal table order, spell the answer.

### The Aha

The Pulling metals -- the metals that ATTRACT, that DRAW THINGS TOGETHER -- spell a word meaning "to bring together." The metals that pull are the ones that [answer]. The thematic resonance: Preservation's power works through Allomancy (end-positive, giving power), and the Pulling metals are the ones that embody attraction/gathering. They have been spelling [the answer] all along.
