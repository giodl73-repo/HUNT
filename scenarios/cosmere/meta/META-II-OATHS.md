## Thematic Frame

The Knights Radiant are Honor's representatives in the Physical Realm. They swear Oaths -- the Immortal Words -- that bind them to a Shard's purpose and grant them power. A Radiant is not a soldier or a mage: a Radiant is an emissary of the divine. The Oaths transform a person into a messenger, a representative, a carrier of Honor's Intent into the world.

The round meta answer describes what a Radiant becomes when they have spoken enough Oaths. Not a warrior. Not a hero. An [answer] -- one sent with a message.

### The Aha

The Orders whose Oaths are most fully known -- whose truth has been most completely spoken in the published books -- are the ones who can contribute their truth to the meta. The deeper an Order has sworn (more Ideals documented), the deeper the extraction reaches into their answer word.

"The more you swear, the deeper the truth runs. Only those who have spoken enough of their truth can speak it here."

The five contributing Orders (Windrunners, Skybreakers, Edgedancers, Lightweavers, Bondsmiths) are the five whose Ideals Sanderson has revealed most fully. This is a meta-textual aha: the puzzle's structure mirrors the real-world state of the published books.
