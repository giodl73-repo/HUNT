## Why These 4 Missing Shards

The 4 missing Shards (Ambition, Dominion, Invention, Mercy) are chosen because:

1. **They are among the least-documented Shards** — Mercy, Invention, and Ambition have unknown or uncertain Vessels, home worlds, and magic systems. Dominion has a known Vessel (Skai) but is Splintered and its effects are merged with Devotion into the Dor.

2. **The 12 represented Shards include all the major, well-characterized ones** — Honor, Cultivation, Odium (the Roshar trio), Preservation, Ruin, Harmony (the Scadrial trio), Devotion, Endowment, Autonomy (the standalone-world Shards), plus Valor, Whimsy, and Virtuosity (the newer reveals).

3. **No feeder puzzle needs to stretch to reference these 4** — every puzzle in Modules E and F can naturally reference one of the 12 represented Shards without forced connections.

4. **The anagram works** — A, D, I, M uniquely anagram to a thematically perfect word.

## 80% Rule Check

| Scenario | Effect | Can Solve? |
|----------|--------|------------|
| All 12 feeders solved, all 12 Shards identified | 4 missing = A, D, I, M → anagram → answer | Yes |
| 11 of 12 feeders solved | 5 missing Shards (4 correct + 1 extra). Pool of 5 first letters still contains A, D, I, M. | Very likely — answer is findable within the 5-letter pool |
| 10 of 12 feeders solved | 6 missing Shards. First-letter pool = 6 letters. | Likely — A, D, I, M are all present; anagram with thematic clue narrows it |
| 8 of 12 feeders solved | 8 missing Shards. | Harder but thematic clue + known word list makes it possible |

The 4 missing Shards (Ambition, Dominion, Invention, Mercy) are the ones solvers are LEAST likely to identify independently, so even partial solving tends to correctly exclude them. A solver who identifies 10 Shards will almost certainly have the 8 "famous" ones (Honor, Cultivation, Odium, Preservation, Ruin, Harmony, Devotion, Endowment) plus 2 others, meaning the 4 obscure ones remain consistently in the "missing" pool.

**Verdict: Passes the 80% rule.** The answer is robust to partial solving because the 4 missing Shards are precisely the ones solvers are least likely to over-identify.
