# P01 — Burning Through

*Module A (Allomancy) | Iron (#1) | Difficulty: ★★*

---

Sixteen metals burn in a Mistborn's stomach. Each grants a different power. Each power is described below in the words of those who have burned them — though the metal's name has been excised.

One word in each description carries more weight than the rest. It is marked. The marks are not decoration.

---

## The Sixteen Burns

Pulls on nearby metals, attracting **them** toward the Allomancer.

Pushes on nearby metals — a **hard** force repelling them away from the Allomancer.

Heightens all five senses. **Every** sound, scent, taste, touch, and sight grows sharper.

Enhances physical strength, speed, balance, and endurance beyond any **usual** limit.

Riots emotions in others, inflaming **passion** until it overtakes reason.

Soothes emotions in others, leaving the target **relaxed** and compliant.

Creates an **invisible** cloud that hides Allomantic pulses from those who seek.

A **sense** for the burning of others. Detects nearby Allomantic pulses.

Shows alternate past selves — the life **each** burner might have led.

Shows the burner's **own** immediate future. A few seconds of possible action.

Slows time in a bubble. The world beyond the **fringe** races ahead.

Speeds time in a bubble. The world outside **moves** as if through amber.

Wipes **every** metal reserve the burner holds. Nothing remains.

Burns all current metals in a single massive flare. **Tremendous** power, gone in an instant.

On contact, wipes **another** person's metal reserves entirely. A touch and they are empty.

On contact, causes another person's metals to flare in a **luminous** burst.

---

## The Extraction

The metals have a canonical order — first to sixteenth, as the table has always arranged them. A solver who knows which metal matches which mark can place them in their proper positions.

The marked words, read first through sixteenth, form a sentence. The answer hides within it. Six letters.

---

*Some things burn clean. Some leave residue. The residue is the point.*
