# P02 — Alloy Partners

*Module A (Allomancy) | Steel (#2) | Difficulty: ★★*

---

Every base metal has a partner. The partner is its alloy. They share a quadrant, share a row, share a destiny — but never a power.

Eight riddles follow. Each describes a pair: the base metal's Allomantic gift and the alloy's Feruchemical store. Name both metals in each pair.

---

## The Eight Pairings

**i.** Nullifying another's reserves with a touch, its partner stores the very essence of power — Investiture in its rawest form.

**ii.** Finding its match in something so basic: one pulls metals close, the other stores the speed to outrun them.

**iii.** Echoes of past selves haunt the one who burns it. Its partner stores the determination to keep going despite such visions.

**iv.** One heightens every sense to painful clarity. The other stores raw strength — the body's blunt answer to a sharpened mind.

**v.** Dilated time, stretched thin in a bubble of slowness. Its partner stores the energy to endure such hunger.

**vi.** Riotous emotion follows in its wake — inflamed passions, stoked fury. Its partner stores a gentler thing: warmth against the cold.

**vii.** Guarded behind a cloud of silence, hidden from all seekers. Its partner stores the opposite of forgetting — wakefulness, sharp and unyielding.

**viii.** Instant obliteration of one's own reserves, a scorched-earth burn. Its partner stores Connection — the bonds that tie souls across the Cosmere.

---

## The Extraction

Eight pairs, eight riddles. The pairs have a natural order — the base metal's position in the Allomantic table, lowest to highest.

Arrange the riddles in that order. Read the first letter of each riddle's opening word, first pair through eighth. The phrase that forms contains the answer. Six letters.

---

*A metal alone is raw. A metal with its partner is something else entirely.*
