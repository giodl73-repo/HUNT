# P03 — Feruchemical Reserves

*Module A (Allomancy) | Zinc (#5) | Difficulty: ★★★*

---

A Feruchemist stores. That is the bargain — suffer now, benefit later. Each metal holds a different attribute. The cost of storing is always the opposite of the reward.

Sixteen metalminds, sixteen costs. Each description below captures what a Feruchemist endures while filling a metalmind. Name the metal. The first words carry more than meaning.

---

## The Sixteen Stores

Flesh grows lighter with each step. The metalmind drinks density, and the body floats toward irrelevance.

In a blur the world races past. Legs refuse to keep pace. Speed pours out of muscle and into metal.

Narrowing down to nothing — one sense dims, then vanishes. The metalmind hoards what the body can no longer perceive.

Draining the sinew, the arms hang limp. Strength departs in a slow tide, stored for a day the body is not yet living.

Thought slows to a crawl. Ideas that once sparked now smolder. The mind pays its tax in lethargy.

Heat bleeds from the skin. Cold settles into the bones like an unwanted guest who means to stay the winter.

Every memory, filed away and forgotten. The mind becomes a blank page — the price of perfect recall later.

Alertness fades into a fog. The eyelids grow heavy. Wakefulness drains into the metalmind one heartbeat at a time.

No wound heals. A cut stays open. A bruise deepens. The body forgets how to mend itself while the metalmind learns.

Certainty drains away. Purpose wavers. The will to act crumbles, stored as raw determination for a future crisis.

Holding each breath becomes a negotiation. The lungs rebel. Air itself seems thinner while the metalmind fills.

One's stomach turns hollow. Hunger gnaws from the inside. Every calorie, every ounce of energy, funnels into the metal.

Relinquishing the self — identity dissolves into fog. Names, preferences, convictions slip away. The metalmind holds what the mind releases.

No bond persists. Language falters, relationships feel distant, the threads of Connection unravel one by one.

Odds turn sour. Coins land wrong. Doors that should open stay shut. Fortune bleeds quietly into the metalmind.

With Investiture draining away, the Feruchemist's very essence of power thins. The metalmind stores what most cannot even name.

---

## The Extraction

Each attribute belongs to one metal. The metals have a table order, first through sixteenth.

Place the descriptions in that order. Read the first letter of each description's opening word, first through sixteenth. The phrase that forms contains the answer. Six letters.
