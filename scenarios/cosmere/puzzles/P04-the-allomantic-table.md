# P04 — The Allomantic Table

*Module A (Allomancy) | Brass (#6) | Difficulty: ★★★*

---

There is a grid. Four quadrants, two axes, sixteen cells. The structure existed before the words for it did. Every metal has a place. Every place has exactly one metal.

Below is the empty grid. Below that, sixteen statements. Each names a quadrant and a direction — Pulling or Pushing — but not whether the metal acts inward or outward. A metal that targets the Allomancer's own body or mind is Internal. A metal that reaches beyond the Allomancer to affect the world is External.

Place each metal where it belongs.

## The Sixteen Statements

**1.** The crowd turns. Mental, Pulling. Emotions inflame. A whisper becomes a roar.

**2.** Roads not taken. Temporal, Pulling. The burner sees alternate selves — echoes of who they might have been.

**3.** Luminous clarity. Physical, Pulling. Every sound, scent, taste, touch, and color sharpens beyond comfort.

**4.** Nudge into oblivion. Enhancement, Pushing. One touch and another Allomancer's burning metals erupt into a single devastating flare.

**5.** Aperture of slowness. Temporal, Pulling. Time crawls within the bubble. The world beyond races ahead.

**6.** Ferrous call. Physical, Pulling. Nearby metals rush toward the Allomancer. The world obeys.

**7.** X marks this cell. Enhancement, Pulling. One touch strips another Allomancer's metal reserves bare.

**8.** Even seekers lose the trail. Mental, Pulling. A cloud of silence descends, hiding all Allomantic pulses.

**9.** Limitless endurance. Physical, Pushing. Strength, speed, and balance surge beyond human measure.

**10.** Wildfire. Enhancement, Pushing. Every burning metal the Allomancer holds flares at once — tremendous power, then nothing.

**11.** Hush. Mental, Pushing. Passions dampen. The furious grow calm. The grieving grow numb.

**12.** Time inverts. Temporal, Pushing. The bubble accelerates. The world outside slows to amber.

**13.** Obliteration from within. Enhancement, Pulling. Every metal reserve the Allomancer holds is annihilated in an instant.

**14.** Its push mirrors the pull. Physical, Pushing. Metals fly away from the Allomancer at speed.

**15.** Imminence. Temporal, Pushing. The burner's own future, seconds away, shimmers into view.

**16.** Muffled pulses ring clear to this one. Mental, Pushing. A sense for burning — a seeker's gift, piercing every cloud.

## The Extraction

The grid reads in a natural order: left to right, top to bottom. Pulling before Pushing. External before Internal. Physical first, Enhancement last. Sixteen cells.

Each cell maps to one statement. Read the first letter of each statement's opening word, in grid order — first cell through sixteenth. The phrase that forms contains the answer. Six letters.
