# P06 — The Coppercloud

*Module A (Allomancy) | Bronze (#8) | Difficulty: ★★★★*

---

Eight Allomancers sit in a row at a long table. Seats one through eight, left to right. Each burns a different metal. One of them is a Smoker — the Coppercloud covers that seat and the seats immediately to its left and right. Anyone inside the cloud is invisible to a Seeker.

A Seeker sits among them. Their report, combined with what is known about each Allomancer, is enough to determine who sits where and who burns what.

---

## The Metals in Play

Eight metals from the Physical and Mental quadrants:

| Metal | Misting Name | Quadrant | Int/Ext | Push/Pull |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Iron | Lurcher | Physical | External | Pull |
| Steel | Coinshot | Physical | External | Push |
| Tin | Tineye | Physical | Internal | Pull |
| Pewter | Pewterarm | Physical | Internal | Push |
| Zinc | Rioter | Mental | External | Pull |
| Brass | Soother | Mental | External | Push |
| Copper | Smoker | Mental | Internal | Pull |
| Bronze | Seeker | Mental | Internal | Push |

---

## The Seeker's Report

The Seeker detects Allomantic pulses from exactly four seats: **1, 2, 6, and 8**.

No pulses detected from seats 3, 4, or 5 — those are hidden by the Coppercloud. The Seeker does not detect their own pulse.

---

## What Is Known

**About the table:**
- The Smoker sits at seat 4. The Coppercloud covers seats 3, 4, and 5.
- No two Physical-quadrant burners sit in adjacent seats.
- Exactly two Physical-quadrant burners are hidden by the Coppercloud.
- The Lurcher (Iron) and the Coinshot (Steel) are on the same side of the cloud — both hidden or both exposed.
- The Coinshot (Steel) sits at a higher-numbered seat than the Lurcher (Iron).
- The Rioter (Zinc) sits at a lower-numbered seat than the Soother (Brass).
- The Tineye (Tin) is hidden by the Coppercloud.

**About the Allomancers:**
- **Delin** burns one of the two Internal Mental metals.
- **Demoux** burns a Physical-quadrant metal and sits at a lower-numbered seat than Eveni.
- **Eveni** burns a Pushing metal.
- **Hadrel** sits at one end of the table and burns a Pulling metal.
- **Idara** sits at seat 2.
- **Noorden** burns an External Mental metal.
- **Sorben** is not hidden by the Coppercloud.
- **Teven** sits at seat 7.

---

## The Extraction

Once all eight seats are filled — name and metal — read the first letter of each Allomancer's name, seat one through seat six. Six letters. The answer describes what the Coppercloud provides.

---

*Eight Allomancers. Eight metals. One cloud. In the silence beneath the copper, everything becomes clear — if one knows where to listen.*
