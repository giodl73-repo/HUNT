# P10 — The Survivor's Legacy

**Chromium (#15)** · Module B (Scadrial History) · Round 1

---

> The Survivor dies once. It does not take. Three hundred years later his fingerprints remain on everything — every law, every church, every quiet arrangement made in back rooms. Eight threads stretch from his Era to the next. Match each cause to its consequence, name the bridge between them, and the Survivor's true nature reveals itself.

---

## The Puzzle

Eight actions taken by Kelsier (or directly set in motion by him) during Era 1 are listed in the left column. Eight consequences that manifest in Era 2 are listed in the right column. They are not aligned. Match each cause to its effect. Then identify the single word that best names the connection — the concept that bridges three centuries.

Order the eight matched pairs by the chronological date of the Era 1 cause (earliest first). Read the first letter of each connection word, top to bottom. The answer hides within the resulting string.

---

### Causes (Era 1)

| # | Cause |
|---|-------|
| **C1** | Kelsier escaped the Pits of Hathsin — the only prisoner in a thousand years to do so. The man who walked out was no longer the man who walked in. |
| **C2** | Kelsier recruited a crew: a Thug, a Soother, a Smoker, a Seeker, a Tineye, a skaa organizer, and a street urchin who turned out to be Mistborn. |
| **C3** | Kelsier's rebellion shattered the political order. Noble houses that had ruled for centuries found their foundations cracking. The skaa began to see themselves as something other than property. |
| **C4** | Kelsier spent months training a young street girl to burn metals, teaching her Allomantic combat, and — perhaps more importantly — teaching her to trust. |
| **C5** | Kelsier was killed by the Lord Ruler in the streets of Luthadel. He did not run. He did not hide. He died where every skaa in the city could see. |
| **C6** | Kelsier's death set a chain in motion: the girl he trained confronted the Lord Ruler alone and ended a thousand-year empire by pulling free a pair of metal bracers. |
| **C7** | Kelsier's rebellion created the conditions for a Terris Keeper to stand at the end of all things and pick up two Shards that no one else could hold. |
| **C8** | Kelsier refused to pass on. His mind persisted in the Cognitive Realm — a ghost with ambition, tethered to the world by sheer stubbornness. |
