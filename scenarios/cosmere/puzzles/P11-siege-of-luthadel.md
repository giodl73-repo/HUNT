# P11 — The Siege of Luthadel

**Bendalloy (#12)** · Module B (Scadrial History) · Round 1

---

> Three armies. One capital. No one inside the walls asked for this. The siege of Luthadel is a problem with exactly one solution — though it took a god's death and a street girl's courage to find it. The facts are here, stripped to logic. The rest is deduction.

---

## The Puzzle

During the events of The Well of Ascension, three armies converged on Luthadel. Each army differs from the others in six respects. Assign the three options in each row to the correct army. The clues below determine every cell.

---

### The Armies

| Code | Army |
|------|------|
| **V** | **Venture's Host** — commanded by Straff Venture, Elend's father |
| **C** | **Cett's Force** — commanded by Ashweather Cett, warlord and opportunist |
| **K** | **The Koloss Horde** — blue-skinned Hemalurgic creatures, four spikes each |

---

### Properties (assign one option per army in each row)

| # | Property | Option 1 | Option 2 | Option 3 |
|---|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Identifying color | BLUE | CRIMSON | GOLD |
| 2 | Commander's driving trait | GREED | PRIDE | RAGE |
| 3 | Troops' origin | ENGINEERED | ENLISTED | MERCENARY |
| 4 | Allomantic support | ABSENT | STRONG | SUBTLE |
| 5 | Battlefield strategy | CHARGE | OPPORTUNISM | SIEGE |
| 6 | What the army left behind | CUNNING | DISCIPLINE | HAVOC |

---

### Clues

**A.** The army whose troops were ENGINEERED shared their identifying color with their own skin.

**B.** The force motivated by GREED did not besiege — it waited. Its strategy was OPPORTUNISM.

**C.** The MERCENARY companies served under the commander whose legacy was CUNNING. That commander was not Straff.

**D.** The army with STRONG Allomantic support chose a SIEGE strategy. It was not the army driven by GREED.

**E.** The army that CHARGED was also the army with ABSENT Allomantic support. Its troops were not ENLISTED.

**F.** Straff's troops were ENLISTED, his banners GOLD, and his most prized Allomancer was a Mistborn son named Zane.

**G.** The commander driven by PRIDE left behind DISCIPLINE. His forces held formation when lesser armies would have broken.

**H.** The army driven by RAGE was the same army identified by the color BLUE.

**I.** The force whose legacy was HAVOC had no Allomancer to hold them in check. Their strategy was the simplest of the three.
