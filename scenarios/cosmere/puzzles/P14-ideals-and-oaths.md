# P14 — Ideals and Oaths

*Module C (Knights Radiant) | Edgedancers (#4) | Difficulty: ★★★*

---

Every Radiant speaks the words. Some speak them in the heat of battle. Some speak them in the silence of confession. All of them mean it, or the words do not work.

Six fragments survive below — each with a word excised. The Order that spoke each Oath is left for the reader to determine. The marked position within each missing word carries the weight.

---

---

## The Rule of Ordering

The Ideals are numbered. The First Ideal stands alone at the beginning. The Second Ideals follow, arranged by Order number, lowest first. The Third Ideals follow after, again by Order number.

Read the marked letters in that sequence. Six letters. One word.
