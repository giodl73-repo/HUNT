# P15 — Spren Bonds

*Module C (Knights Radiant) | Truthwatchers (#5) | Difficulty: ★★★★*

---

A spren chooses. A spren reflects. The nature of the bond is written in behavior — in every argument, every silence, every flash of light or whisper of logic. The ten types of Radiant spren each carry a temperament as distinctive as a fingerprint.

Six bonds are described below. Each ends with a glyph bearing a number of dots. The dots mark a position. The spren type name holds the letter at that position.

---

## Six Bonds

**I.** The spren lectures on propriety between battles. It speaks of honor as a binding obligation, not a feeling. It once refused to manifest as a Blade because its Radiant had spoken a half-truth. Rigid. Unyielding. The oath is everything.

*Glyph: six dots*

---

**II.** This one flickers. It crackles at the edges of perception like embers lifting from a dying fire. It is drawn to destruction the way a moth is drawn to flame — not recklessly, but with a connoisseur's appreciation. When its Radiant exercises restraint, the spren dims. When control slips, it blazes.

*Glyph: five dots*

---

**III.** It stands at attention. Always. This spren does not bend, does not negotiate, does not acknowledge mitigating circumstances. It evaluates every action against a code — not a personal code, but the law as written. When its Radiant acts outside that code, the spren simply waits. It does not argue. It does not need to.

*Glyph: two dots*

---

**IV.** The spren glows faintly at the edges, like light trapped in glass. It is cautious around other spren, preferring the Cognitive Realm where its kind trade in ideas and currents. It has the demeanor of a traveling merchant — open to passage, wary of permanence.

*Glyph: seven dots*

---

**V.** It reasons. Coldly. It follows syllogisms the way other spren follow emotions. Black as ink, sharp as a nib, it has no patience for sentiment. When its Radiant reaches a conclusion through intuition rather than logic, the spren requests the proof.

*Glyph: one dot*

---

**VI.** This spren took root slowly. It grew alongside its Radiant — not from a single dramatic moment, but from a long season of nurture. It tends to the wounded before speaking of oaths, and measures progress not in power but in whether new growth has taken hold.

*Glyph: four dots*
