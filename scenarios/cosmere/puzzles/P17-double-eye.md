# P17 — The Double Eye

*Module C (Knights Radiant) | Windrunners (#1) | Difficulty: ★★★★*

---

The ten Orders form a wheel. Each spoke is a Surge. Each Surge is shared by exactly two adjacent Orders. The pattern is called the Double Eye of the Almighty, and it has not changed since the founding.

Ten clues describe the Surges. Each Surge sits on the spoke between two Orders. A second signal — a Herald, a gemstone, a spren — confirms which side of the spoke to read. Place all ten Orders in their ring positions.

Five points on the wheel are infused. At each, the Stormlight reaches a depth — one letter or two letters into the Order name. Read the infused letters, starting from the arc nearest the Soldier's seat and moving clockwise.

## The Confirmations

For five of the ten positions, a secondary signal identifies which Order sits there. For the remaining five, the ring constraints determine placement once the confirmed Orders are fixed.

**Position 1:** The spren here are named for a virtue. They are rigid in character and will not accept a bond with one who lies.

**Position 4:** This Order's Herald is called the Healer. The gemstone is colorless and flawless.

**Position 5:** The spren here are sometimes mistaken for fog. Their Herald is the Scholar.

**Position 9:** The Herald of this Order endured millennia of solitary torment. The gemstone is yellow.

**Position 10:** Only three spren of this kind exist. One is the Stormfather. One is the Nightwatcher. One is the Sibling.
