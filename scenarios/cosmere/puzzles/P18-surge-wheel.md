# P18 — The Surge Wheel

*Module C (Knights Radiant) | Lightweavers (#6) | Difficulty: ★★★★*

---

Ten forces govern the world. Each is shared by two. The Orders sit on a wheel and each pair of neighbors shares exactly one force between them. This is the architecture of the Double Eye — not a metaphor, but a blueprint.

The wheel below has ten seats and ten spokes. Each spoke carries a Surge name. The seat at the top is claimed: the Order whose members fly and protect sits there. The remaining nine seats are open.

Place the ten Orders. Then trace what the wheel reveals.

---

## The Placement Rules

1. Windrunners occupy seat [1] at the top. Their Surges are Adhesion and Gravitation.
2. Each remaining seat must be filled with one Order such that the two Surges on its adjacent spokes match that Order's two Surges.
3. There is only one valid arrangement.

---

## The Extraction

Seven seats bear a mark of Stormlight — a glyph inscribed on the wheel by someone who knew its design. Each glyph carries a number: the depth to read.
