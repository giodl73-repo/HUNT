# P20 — Bridge Four

**Windrunners** · Module D · Round 2 (Roshar)

---

> They carried a bridge. Then they carried each other. Nine names from that crew are scrambled below, but something else has infiltrated the ranks. Six of the nine carry an extra letter — a stowaway that does not belong. Three are clean. The strays, read in order, have something to say.

---

## The Puzzle

Each entry is a scrambled version of a Bridge Four member's name. Six entries have **one extra letter** inserted that is not part of the name. Three entries are **clean anagrams** with no extra letter.

Unscramble each name. Identify the extra letter in each infiltrated entry. Read the six extra letters top to bottom.
