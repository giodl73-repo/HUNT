# P23 — Fabrial Workshop

**Bondsmiths** · Module D · Round 2 (Roshar)

---

> Navani Kholin traps spren in gemstones and calls it scholarship. Others call it cruelty. Either way, the devices work. Seven fabrials sit on the workshop bench. Each one does something. Each one holds a gem. Each gem carries a name tied to an Essence of Roshar. Match the device to its stone. A spanreed writes because its ruby remembers the spark of connection. Every gemstone-effect pairing follows the same logic. The stones have something to say.

---

## The Puzzle

Seven fabrial effects are described. For each, determine:
- **Which Polestone** (gemstone) powers it, based on the stone's **Essence** — the aspect of the natural world it governs

Then take the letter at the indicated position from the **gemstone name**.

The ten Polestones and their Essences: Sapphire governs Zephyr (wind). Smokestone governs Vapor. Ruby governs Spark (fire). Diamond governs Lucentia (crystal, glass). Emerald governs Pulp (wood, plants, grain). Garnet governs Blood. Zircon governs Tallow (oil, wax). Amethyst governs Foil (metal). Topaz governs Talus (stone). Heliodor governs Sinew (flesh, muscle).
