# P30 — The Shattering

*Module E (The 16 Shards) | Virtuosity | Difficulty: ★★★★★*

---

Before everything, sixteen conspirators stood in a circle. A seventeenth watched from outside. He did not take a piece. He has spent a very long time regretting nothing about that choice. Or so he claims.

The arrangement below is hypothetical. One does not claim to remember exactly where everyone stood — though one does, in fact, remember. The clues are consistent with what is known, and only one arrangement satisfies all of them.

Sixteen seats in a circle, numbered 1 through 16 clockwise. Ten Vessels are known by name. Six are lost. Place the ten named Vessels. The six empty seats belong to the forgotten.

The seventeenth watched from outside the circle, standing between seats 16 and 1. Six seats are marked with an extraction position. For each, write the Vessel name and take the letter at the given position. Read the six letters clockwise — seat 1 first, then onward — to discover where the Shattering began.

## Placement Clues

**Adjacencies:**

1. Aona and Skai stood beside each other. Partners before and after the breaking. Aona holds the lower-numbered seat.

2. Ati and Leras stood beside each other. Opposites who chose to work together. Ati holds the lower-numbered seat.

3. Koravellium Avast stood directly beside Bavadin.

**Proximity:**

4. Between Skai and Rayse, exactly one seat sits empty. The destroyer stood near his future victims. Rayse holds the higher-numbered seat.

5. Between Bavadin and Ulas Dal, exactly one seat sits empty. The isolationist and the ambitious, divided by a single gap.

**Separations:**

6. Tanavast and Rayse are exactly 5 seats apart along the shorter arc of the circle. They who would become the bitterest of enemies did not stand close.

7. Ati and Edgli are exactly 3 seats apart (shorter arc).

**Placement:**

8. Tanavast does not sit beside Koravellium Avast. That alliance formed later.

9. Leras holds a seat numbered between 5 and 10 (inclusive).

10. Ulas Dal holds a seat numbered 13 or higher.

11. Edgli holds a seat numbered between 7 and 12 (inclusive).

**Anchor:**

12. Aona holds seat 1. The seventeenth stood directly to her left.

13. Koravellium Avast holds an even-numbered seat.

**Structure:**

14. No two unknown Vessels sit in adjacent seats. Each gap in memory is separated by at least one known name.
