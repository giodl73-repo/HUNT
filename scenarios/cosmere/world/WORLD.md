# THE SIXTEENTH SHARD — World Overview

## Domain

The Cosmere is Brandon Sanderson's shared universe — a collection of planets, each with its own magic system, all connected by an underlying metaphysical framework. The magic systems are powered by fragments of a shattered god (Adonalsium), and a handful of immortal Worldhoppers travel between the planets.

This hunt draws from the two most developed Cosmere worlds (Scadrial and Roshar) and the overarching Cosmere framework (the Shards, the Shattering, Hoid's journey).

## Content Rules

1. All facts must come from **published Sanderson works** or the **Coppermind wiki** (community-verified canon).
2. No Words of Brandon (WoBs) unless confirmed in published text.
3. No unpublished drafts, fan theories, or speculation presented as fact.
4. Items flagged with **[VERIFY]** require confirmation before use in puzzles. A puzzle author must not build a puzzle mechanic on a [VERIFY] fact without first resolving it.
5. Puzzle authors verify all facts against these world/ files. Not memory. Not each other's puzzles.

## Cross-References

The systems interlock heavily. Key cross-references for puzzle design:

- **Allomancy table ↔ Shards:** 16 metals map to the 16 Shards (both are 4x4 structures)
- **Knights Radiant ↔ Roshar:** The 10 orders reference Heralds, Gemstones, and locations from roshar.md
- **Hoid ↔ Everything:** Hoid appears in works spanning Scadrial, Roshar, Nalthis, Sel, and more
- **Shards ↔ Scadrial:** Preservation and Ruin created Scadrial; Harmony (their combination) governs Era 2
- **Shards ↔ Roshar:** Honor, Cultivation, and Odium are the three Shards on Roshar
- **Allomancy ↔ Scadrial:** The metals table IS the Scadrial magic system; characters are defined by which metals they can use
