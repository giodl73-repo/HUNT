# Hoid — Appearances Across the Cosmere

Hoid (also known as Cephandrius, Topaz, Wit, and many other names) is a Worldhopper who appears in every major Cosmere work. He was present at the Shattering of Adonalsium but did not take a Shard. He is functionally immortal, possesses abilities from multiple magic systems, and collects Investiture types as he travels between worlds.
