# Knights Radiant — The 10 Orders

The Knights Radiant are an order of warriors on Roshar who bond with spren to gain Surgebinding abilities. Each order has access to 2 of the 10 Surges, bonds with a specific type of spren, and is associated with one of the 10 Heralds and one of the 10 Polestones (gemstones).

---

## The 10 Surges

Surges are the fundamental forces of Roshar. Each Surge is shared by exactly two orders.

| # | Surge | Description | Shared By |
|---|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | **Adhesion** | Binds objects together; the Surge of Pressure and Vacuum. Full Lashings. Spiritual Adhesion can bind people (Connection). | Windrunners, Bondsmiths |
| 2 | **Gravitation** | Manipulates gravitational pull. Basic Lashing (change direction of gravity for a person or object). | Windrunners, Skybreakers |
| 3 | **Division** | Destroys, burns, degrades. Can cause objects to burn orite [VERIFY exact mechanics]. | Skybreakers, Dustbringers |
| 4 | **Abrasion** | Manipulates friction. Edgedancer slickness (frictionless movement). | Dustbringers, Edgedancers |
| 5 | **Progression** | Growth and healing. Can grow plants rapidly, heal injuries. Regrowth. | Edgedancers, Truthwatchers |
| 6 | **Illumination** | Creates illusions — visual and auditory. Lightweaving. | Truthwatchers, Lightweavers |
| 7 | **Transformation** | Soulcasting — changes one substance into another by persuading an object's soul in the Cognitive Realm. | Lightweavers, Elsecallers |
| 8 | **Transportation** | Movement between the Physical, Cognitive, and Spiritual Realms. Elsecalling. | Elsecallers, Willshapers |
| 9 | **Cohesion** | Manipulates the molecular structure of solid objects (reshaping stone with hands, for example). | Willshapers, Stonewards |
| 10 | **Tension** | Manipulates the stiffness/rigidity of objects (making something rigid or flexible). | Stonewards, Bondsmiths |

## Surge Sharing Pattern

The Surges form a ring — each Surge is shared by two adjacent orders. The orders form a decagonal (10-sided) pattern called the Double Eye of the Almighty:

```
        Windrunners
       /            \
Bondsmiths        Skybreakers
    |                  |
Stonewards       Dustbringers
    |                  |
Willshapers      Edgedancers
       \            /
  Elsecallers  Truthwatchers
        \      /
      Lightweavers
```

Each line connecting two orders represents a shared Surge.

## The Ideals (Immortal Words)

Each Radiant swears up to 5 Ideals (also called Oaths). The First Ideal is universal to all orders. Subsequent Ideals are order-specific.

### The First Ideal (Universal)

> "Life before death. Strength before weakness. Journey before destination."

### Order-Specific Ideals

**Windrunners:**
- 2nd: "I will protect those who cannot protect themselves."
- 3rd: "I will protect even those I hate, so long as it is right."
- 4th: Accepting that you cannot save everyone. (Kaladin's 4th: "I accept that there will be those I cannot protect.") [VERIFY exact wording]
- 5th: Not yet spoken in published works.

**Skybreakers:**
- 2nd: Choose a code of law or a person to follow as law.
- 3rd: Swear to dedicate yourself to a cause of justice. ("I swear to follow the will of Dalinar Kholin.") [VERIFY — Szeth's specific oath]
- 4th: Become the law — internalize justice.
- 5th: Become the law itself — the Skybreaker IS the law. (Nale has sworn the 5th.) [VERIFY]

**Dustbringers:**
- 2nd-5th: Not fully documented in published works. Related to self-control and responsible use of destructive power. [VERIFY]

**Edgedancers:**
- 2nd: "I will remember those who have been forgotten."
- 3rd: "I will listen to those who have been ignored."
- 4th-5th: Not yet spoken in published works. [VERIFY]

**Truthwatchers:**
- 2nd-5th: Not fully documented. Related to seeking and sharing truth. Renarin's oaths are unusual due to his corrupted spren. [VERIFY]

**Lightweavers:**
- Lightweavers do not swear traditional oaths. Instead, they speak Truths — deeply personal admissions about themselves.
- 1st Truth: The First Ideal (universal).
- 2nd Truth: A personal truth the Lightweaver has been hiding from themselves. (Shallan's: "I killed my father." / "I killed my mother.")
- 3rd Truth: A deeper personal truth. (Shallan's 3rd: "I am not that child anymore." / varies by interpretation) [VERIFY exact progression]
- 4th-5th: Additional Truths, increasingly difficult to face.

**Elsecallers:**
- 2nd-5th: Not fully documented. Related to self-improvement and reaching potential. [VERIFY]

**Willshapers:**
- 2nd-5th: Not fully documented. Related to personal freedom and liberation. [VERIFY]

**Stonewards:**
- 2nd-5th: Not fully documented. Related to being dependable and never giving up. [VERIFY]

**Bondsmiths:**
- 2nd: "I will unite instead of divide. I will bring men together."
- 3rd: "I will take responsibility for what I have done. If I must fall, I will rise each time a better man." (Dalinar's)
- 4th: Dalinar's 4th Ideal involves accepting the responsibility of Connection and the power it grants. [VERIFY exact wording]
- 5th: Not yet spoken.

## Puzzle Affordances

- **10 orders x 2 Surges** — each Surge shared by exactly 2 orders; ring topology
- **Herald-Order-Gemstone triples** — matching/deduction puzzles
- **The Oaths** — sequential, extractable text. The First Ideal is 10 words.
- **Spren types** — 10 unique names (Honorspren, Cryptics, etc.)
- **Double Eye pattern** — the decagonal layout is a visual structure
- **Gemstone colors and Essences** — secondary encoding layer
- **Order numbering** — 1-10 mapping (Windrunners=1 through Bondsmiths=10)
