# Roshar — World Data

Roshar is the planet of the Stormlight Archive. It is home to three Shards: Honor (Splintered), Cultivation (active), and Odium (trapped, held by Taravangian as of Rhythm of War). The primary magic system is Surgebinding, powered by Stormlight captured in gemstones during Highstorms.

The detailed Knights Radiant data is in `knights-radiant.md`.

## The 10 Heralds

The Heralds were bound by the Oathpact — an agreement with Honor. When a Desolation threatened Roshar, the Heralds would return from Braize (the planet where Odium's forces were imprisoned between Desolations) to lead humanity. ### The Oathpact

- The Heralds agreed to return to Braize after each Desolation to hold back the Fused
- If a Herald broke under torture, a new Desolation would begin
- After the Aharietiam (the Last Desolation, ~4,500 years before the main story), 9 of 10 Heralds abandoned the Oathpact, leaving only Taln
- This is called the **Day of Recreance** — the Knights Radiant also broke their oaths and abandoned their Shardblades and Shardplate [VERIFY — the Recreance was related but not the same event as the Herald abandonment. ## Highstorms and the Everstorm

### Highstorms
- Massive storms that sweep across Roshar from east to west
- Carry Stormlight — gemstones left exposed during a Highstorm are infused with Stormlight
- The Stormfather (a massive spren, a remnant/splinter of Honor) rides the Highstorm
- Highstorms come regularly but not perfectly predictably; stormwardens predict their timing
- The eastern side of Roshar (the Shattered Plains) is more storm-battered; the western side (Shinovar) is sheltered
- Flora and fauna on Roshar have evolved to retract into shells/rock during Highstorms (rockbuds, cremlings, etc.)

### The Everstorm
- A new storm introduced at the end of Words of Radiance
- Sweeps from west to east (opposite direction of Highstorms)
- Created/summoned by the Parshendi (listeners) through the summoning of a voidspren storm
- Carries Voidlight (Odium's Investiture) instead of Stormlight
- Transforms Parshmen (the docile slave caste) into awakened Singers with Forms of Power
- The Everstorm's existence means Roshar now faces storms from both directions

## Fabrials

Fabrials are devices that use trapped spren and gemstones to produce magical effects. They are Roshar's form of magitech.

### Gemstones and Stormlight
- 10 Polestones (gemstones), each associated with an order and an Essence
- Gemstones hold Stormlight and power fabrials
- Larger, more perfect gems hold more Stormlight
- Stormlight leaks from gemstones over time (not permanent storage, though perfect gems hold it indefinitely)

## Shardblades and Shardplate

### Shardblades
- Magical swords that can cut through anything except living flesh (they cut the soul instead, killing or severing the spiritual connection to limbs)
- **Dead Shardblades:** The abandoned Blades from the Recreance. These are bonded spren whose Radiant broke their oaths, "killing" the spren. They scream when held by a Radiant. Can be bonded by anyone through a week of proximity.
- **Living Shardblades:** Manifested from a living spren bond. Only Radiants have these. They can be summoned and dismissed at will. They do not scream.
- **Honorblades:** The original Blades, wielded by the Heralds. Grant Surgebinding without a spren bond. There are 10, one per Herald.

### Shardplate
- Magical armor of immense strength
- **Dead Shardplate:** Abandoned at the Recreance. Can be worn by anyone.
- **Living Shardplate:** Manifested by 4th-Ideal Radiants. Composed of lesser spren associated with the Radiant's order (e.g., windspren for Windrunners). [VERIFY — Shardplate from lesser spren confirmed in Rhythm of War]
