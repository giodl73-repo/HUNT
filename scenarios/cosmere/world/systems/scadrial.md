# Scadrial — World Data

Scadrial is the planet created jointly by the Shards Preservation and Ruin. It is home to the Metallic Arts: Allomancy (powered by Preservation), Feruchemy (a balance of both Shards), and Hemalurgy (powered by Ruin). The detailed metal tables are in `allomancy.md`.

---

## Historical Eras

### The Final Empire (Era 1) — ~1000 years under the Lord Ruler

**The Lord Ruler (Rashek):**
- A Terris Feruchemist who traveled to the Well of Ascension with Alendi (the supposed Hero of Ages)
- Killed Alendi, took the power at the Well, and reshaped the world
- Both a full Mistborn and full Feruchemist — a **Fullborn**. Used Allomantic and Feruchemical abilities together (compounding) for effective immortality.
- Ruled for 1,024 years as a tyrant-god
- Moved the planet's orbit closer to the sun, then created the ashmounts to produce ash clouds to cool the planet, then created the mistwraiths/kandra to deal with ecological collapse [VERIFY the exact sequence of changes]
- Created the Steel Ministry and the Obligators to administer his empire
- Created the Steel Inquisitors through Hemalurgy

**Social Structure:**
- **Skaa:** The oppressed working class. Descendants of those who did not support Rashek at the Well. Forbidden from interbreeding with nobility (death penalty). Some skaa carry suppressed Allomantic bloodlines.
- **Nobility:** The ruling class. Descendants of those who supported Rashek. Many noble houses have Allomantic bloodlines. Mistings and Mistborn arise from noble families (and secretly from skaa with noble blood).
- **Obligators:** Members of the Steel Ministry. Administrators, bureaucrats, and religious authority. Tattoo-like markings around their eyes.
- **Steel Inquisitors:** Hemalurgically spiked agents of the Lord Ruler. Massive spikes through their eyes. Possess multiple Allomantic and Feruchemical abilities stolen through Hemalurgy. Nearly unkillable (remove the linchpin spike to kill them).
- **Kandra:** Shapeshifters created by the Lord Ruler from mistwraiths. Use Hemalurgic Blessings (two spikes each) for sapience. Can absorb and replicate a dead person's body. Organized into Generations (First Generation oldest).
- **Koloss:** Blue-skinned, increasingly large warriors created through Hemalurgy (four spikes each). Originally human. Used as the Lord Ruler's shock troops.

**The Mists:**
- Dense fog that appears every night across Scadrial
- Preservation's body/influence — the mists ARE Preservation's Investiture in physical form
- During the events of the trilogy, the mists begin appearing during the day ("the Deepness")
- The mists can "Snap" Allomancers — awaken their latent Allomantic abilities (a traumatic process)

## The Metallic Arts Summary

| Art | Powered By | Mechanism | Net Effect |
|-----|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Allomancy** | Preservation | Burn (ingest and metabolize) metal to access power | End-positive (net gain of power from Preservation) |
| **Feruchemy** | Balance of Preservation + Ruin | Store attributes in metalminds; tap later | End-neutral (stores and retrieves same amount) |
| **Hemalurgy** | Ruin | Drive a metal spike through one person to steal abilities, then spike it into another | End-negative (power is lost in transfer; entropy) |

**Compounding:** A Twinborn who has the same metal for both Allomancy and Feruchemy can "compound" — burn a metalmind Allomantically to get a massive Feruchemical output. Example: Miles Hundredlives compounds gold for near-infinite healing.
