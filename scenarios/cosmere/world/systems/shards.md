# The 16 Shards of Adonalsium

Before the Cosmere's recorded history, a god-like entity called **Adonalsium** was Shattered into 16 pieces (Shards) by a group of conspirators at a location called the **Shattering**. Each Shard embodies a single Intent — an aspect of Adonalsium's nature. Sixteen individuals (the **original Vessels**) each took up one Shard.

A Shard's Intent shapes the behavior of whoever holds it. Over millennia, the Vessel's personality merges with the Shard's Intent.

## Shard Interactions

**Opposing Pairs:**
- Preservation and Ruin — directly opposed Intents. Combined by Sazed into Harmony.
- Honor and Odium — thematically opposed on Roshar. Honor was Splintered by Odium.

**Known Combatants (Odium's Shard-killing campaign):**
1. Devotion and Dominion — Splintered on Sel
2. Ambition — Splintered near Threnody
3. Honor — Splintered on Roshar
4. Odium was then trapped in the Rosharan system by the remnants of Honor

**Co-located Shards:**
- Roshar: Honor + Cultivation (+ Odium, trapped)
- Scadrial: Preservation + Ruin (now Harmony)
- Sel: Devotion + Dominion (both Splintered; their power is the Dor)

## The Shattering

**What happened:** Sixteen individuals conspired to kill Adonalsium and divided its power among themselves. Each took a piece — a Shard — defined by a single Intent.

**Key participants mentioned in published works:**
- Hoid was present at the Shattering but **did not take a Shard**. He was offered one and refused (or was unable to take one). His reasons are one of the Cosmere's central mysteries.
- Frost (a dragon) was also aware of the Shattering. He and Hoid correspond via letters (the epigraphs in Stormlight Archive).

## Puzzle Affordances

- **16 Shards = 16 entries** — maps directly to 16 letters (A-P) or to the Allomancy table (16 metals, 16 Shards)
- **Single-word Intent names** — HONOR, RUIN, PRESERVATION, etc. — first letters, word lengths, letter positions all usable
- **Original Vessel names** — a second layer of unique identifiers
- **Status categories** — Splintered, Held, Combined, Uncertain — categorical encoding
- **Planet associations** — each Shard maps to a world/system
- **Investiture types** — each Shard powers a magic system
- **Odium's kill list** — ordered sequence of Shard destructions
- **Missing information** — 4+ Shards with unknown Vessels/worlds; the "holes" in the data are puzzle-useful
