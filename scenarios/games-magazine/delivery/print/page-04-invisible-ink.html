Invisible Ink
★★★★ By R.J. Calder
The best cryptic clues hide their workings in full view — the definition sits beside the wordplay, and you cannot tell where one ends and the other begins. These ten clues take that principle as their subject. Every answer is a word meaning “hidden,” “concealed,” or “secret.” The mechanisms are the usual suspects: containers, hidden words, anagrams, reversals, and deletions. Nothing exotic. Just well-camouflaged.
