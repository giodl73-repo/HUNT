# Lurking Words

**★★★** | By Margaret Huang

Every answer in this crossword has been clued fairly. But six of the Across entries are hiding something — a smaller word lodged inside the larger one, reading left to right, in consecutive letters. The hidden words are not clued. You must find them yourself. When you do, read their first letters from top to bottom. They have something to tell you.
