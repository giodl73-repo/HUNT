# The Vanishing Act

**★★★★★** | By R.J. Calder

A word search. You have done a thousand of them. Twenty words to find, all relating to concealment and illusion. Horizontal, vertical, diagonal, forwards, backwards — the usual rules apply. You will find nineteen of them without difficulty. The twentieth is the one that matters.

## Instructions

Find all 20 words in the grid. Words may run horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, and may read forwards or backwards.

When you have found 19, one word will remain unfound by standard rules. That word IS in the grid — but it follows a path you have not tried yet. The path itself is the puzzle.
