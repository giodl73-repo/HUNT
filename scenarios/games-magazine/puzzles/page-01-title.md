# HIDDEN IN PLAIN SIGHT

*GAMES Magazine — Vol. 47, No. 3*

---

You are surrounded by secrets. Words hide inside other words. Patterns lurk beneath noise. Familiar images conceal unfamiliar shapes. The best hiding places are not dark corners or locked vaults — they are surfaces you look at every day without really seeing.

This issue's puzzle spread celebrates the art of concealment. Eight puzzles, eight ways of hiding something where you would never think to look. A crossword whose answers harbor stowaways. Cryptic clues whose very solutions mean "hidden." A logic grid with a liar in its midst. An illustration teeming with objects you will swear were never there. A redacted essay. A mathematical grid with sequences woven through the noise. And a word search that is not quite what it seems.

Some of these puzzles are gentle — a warm-up, a breather. Others will test every tool in your arsenal. The difficulty ratings (★ to ★★★★★) are honest. Trust them.

One piece of advice: in every puzzle, the answer is already in front of you. You just have not learned to see it yet.

— *The Editors*
