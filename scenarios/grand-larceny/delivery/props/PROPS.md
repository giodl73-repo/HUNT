## Distribution Plan

### At Start (check-in)
- Hand each team their evidence dossier
- Staff instruction: "Your case file, detective. Everything you need is inside. Submit your final answer at the website when you've cracked the case."

### During Hunt
- No triggered deliveries. All materials given at start.
- Staff available for hint requests only.

### End-of-Hunt
- No props to collect (all paper/card, teams keep them)
- Debrief / answer reveal session
