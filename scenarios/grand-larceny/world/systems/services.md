| 10:00 PM | 1F → 2F | Housekeeping | Post-gala cleanup |

## P2 Design Notes

The hotel receipt for P2 should contain all four suspects' receipts. The puzzle is finding the arithmetic anomaly. The receipts above are designed so that:

1. Each receipt's arithmetic is correct (totals add up) — there is no "wrong math" clue
2. The hidden pattern is in the TIMING of charges across all four receipts
3. Charges that occurred during the theft window (9:15-9:25) are suspicious — who was where?
4. The dollar amounts, when processed through a specific arithmetic operation, yield the answer word

The specific extraction mechanism is defined in the puzzle brief (PUZZLES.md).
