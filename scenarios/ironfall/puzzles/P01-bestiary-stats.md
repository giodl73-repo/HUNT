## Extraction

The archive's search system indexed each enemy entry with a tag — one circled letter per name. DarkKnight_97 marked them in chronological order of discovery by the community (1997 through 2002).

| Year | Enemy | Position in name | Letter |
|------|-------|-----------------|--------|
| 1997 | TUNDRA GOLEM | 2nd of "TUNDRA" | U |
| 1998 | SHADE WISP | 1st of "SHADE" | S |
| 1999 | DREAD KNIGHT | 10th of "DREADKNIGHT" | H |
| 2000 | VOLT SPIDER | 9th of "VOLTSPIDER" | E |
| 2001 | FROSTCLAW | 2nd letter | R |
