## Extraction

The archive's search system indexed each enemy entry with a tag — one circled letter per name. DarkKnight_97 marked them in chronological order of discovery by the community (1997 through 2001).

| Year | Enemy | Position in name | Letter |
|------|-------|-----------------|--------|
| 1997 | TUNDRA GOLEM | 2nd of "TUNDRAGOLEM" | U |
| 1998 | EMBERFANG | 2nd of "EMBERFANG" | M |
| 1999 | THORNBACK | 6th of "THORNBACK" | B |
| 2000 | FROSTCLAW | 2nd of "FROSTCLAW" | R |
| 2001 | SHADE WISP | 3rd of "SHADEWISP" | A |

Verification: TUNDRAGOLEM[2]=U, EMBERFANG[2]=M, THORNBACK[6]=B, FROSTCLAW[2]=R, SHADEWISP[3]=A.
