## Garek's Riddle

The Ferryman stands between worlds. He speaks in puzzles.

Garek (posted by LoreHunter, 1999): "I asked Garek to tell me about the world. He gave me five riddles. Each one names a number and points to a place. I wrote them down but never understood what they meant."

DataMiner_X (2000): "The number in each riddle is the key. Count that many letters into the place name. No spaces."

---

### Riddle 1

> "One bolt split the peak. From that scar the tower grew."

**Clue:** One bolt. A tower on a peak. The Storm Tower is in Stormspire Peaks, built where lightning scarred the mountain. The number is **1**. The region is Stormspire Peaks.

---

### Riddle 2

> "Three floors the roots descend. Behind the deepest wall, a glade that no axe touched."

**Clue:** Three dungeon floors beneath trees. Ashveil Woods has 3 floors. Behind a hidden wall on B2, the Whispering Glade holds Lore Tablet #1. The number is **3**. The region is Ashveil Forest.

---

### Riddle 3

> "Seven pillars of fire still stand in the waste. Beneath the last, the true forge waits."

**Clue:** Seven pillars of fire in a wasteland. The Volcanic Depths in Ember Wastes have lava pillars. Beneath them lies the Forge Eternal. The number is **7**. The region is Ember Wastes.

---

### Riddle 4

> "Nine locks guard the room where the maker sleeps. Only his own key opens the ninth."

**Clue:** Nine locks in a dungeon, with the final one requiring a special key. The Iron Citadel's deepest level, Morimoto's Lab at B7, is opened by the Developer's Key. The number is **9**. The region is Iron Citadel.

---

### Riddle 5

> "Five tides the temple sank. The gods went with it."

**Clue:** Five tides. A sunken temple. The Drowned Temple in Sunken Vale has 4 floors of water-level puzzles, plus the secret Abyssal Grotto where the old gods sleep. The number is **5**. The region is Sunken Vale.

## Extraction

Each line identifies a region and gives a number. Count that many letters into the region name (remove spaces, count all letters).

| Line | Region | Full name (no spaces) | Position | Letter |
|------|--------|----------------------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Stormspire Peaks | S-T-O-R-M-S-P-I-R-E-P-E-A-K-S | 1 | **S** |
| 2 | Ashveil Forest | A-S-**H**-V-E-I-L-F-O-R-E-S-T | 3 | **H** |
| 3 | Ember Wastes | E-M-B-E-R-W-**A**-S-T-E-S | 7 | **A** |
| 4 | Iron Citadel | I-R-O-N-C-I-T-A-**D**-E-L | 9 | **D** |
| 5 | Sunken Vale | S-U-N-K-**E**-N-V-A-L-E | 5 | **E** |

Reading in order: S, H, A, D, E.

**Answer: SHADE** (ROT13: FUNQR)

### Extraction Verification

Answer: SHADE. Puzzle slot: Act I, position 4. Super-meta extracts the 4th letter: SHADE[4] = D. D in ROT13 = Q. Matches the extraction table.
