```
  Source: Engineering Operations Manual, Section 4 — Propulsion

  POWER PHASE       EPS DRAW    DESCRIPTION
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Acceleration      180 MW      Peak draw. Drive at maximum thrust output.
                                Full EPS channel saturation. Duration
                                proportional to desired final velocity.

  Cruise            120 MW      Steady-state. Thrust maintains current
                                velocity against residual drag. Sustainable
                                indefinitely at rated power.

  Deceleration      60 MW       Reduced thrust. Drive in braking mode.
                                Some energy recovered to EPS. Duration
                                mirrors acceleration phase for same delta-v.

  Full Stop / Idle  0 MW        Drive offline. No EPS draw. Ship holds
                                current position (drift only — no active
                                station-keeping).

  IDENTIFICATION NOTE:
    Any EPS draw on the impulse channel > 0 MW indicates the drive is
    ONLINE and executing a maneuver. Zero draw confirms drive offline.
    A ship in STATION HOLD should show 0 MW for the entire window.
```
