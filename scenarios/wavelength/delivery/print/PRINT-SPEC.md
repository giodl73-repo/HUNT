# Print Spec -- WAVELENGTH (DJ's Final Playlist)

## Overview

One physical prop: Vee's handwritten playlist card for the final broadcast of The Long Wave on WVLG 92.7 FM. This is the meta puzzle. Teams receive it at the start of the hunt, alongside access to the website.

The card lists all songs played during the final broadcast (organized by set), provides answer blanks for the 6 feeder puzzle words, marks the diagonal extraction positions, and includes Vee's final message (revealed only after solving).

### Production Notes

1. **Aging the paper:** Optionally, brew tea, let it cool, lightly brush or dab the printed card. Let dry flat. This adds an organic warmth that plain cream stock lacks. Do not over-saturate -- a single light pass is enough.

2. **Coffee ring:** If adding, place a slightly damp mug bottom on the upper right corner of the card for 30 seconds. The ring should be subtle, not a distraction. Skip this if it risks smudging the ink.

3. **Folding:** The card can be folded once (hamburger fold) and placed inside an envelope or handed directly. A fold mark in the center adds to the "this was in Vee's booth" feel.

4. **Envelope (optional):** A plain kraft paper envelope, with "THE LONG WAVE -- FINAL PLAYLIST" written on the front in marker. Not required but adds to the physical experience.

5. **Margin notes:** Vee's scrawled margin notes (e.g., "read the notes" next to Set 2, "just words" next to Set 6) are optional flavor. They echo her puzzle intro lines but should not give away the mechanism. Include only if using a handwriting font that looks distinct from the body text.

6. **The carets (^) are red.** They are the only non-brown element on the page. This draws the eye to the extraction mechanism and echoes the ON AIR light from the website.

---

## What the Prop IS

- The meta puzzle. Writing feeder answers in the blanks and reading the diagonal solves the meta.
- A real object from Vee's world. The last playlist she ever wrote.
- The emotional centerpiece. Her final quote lives on this card.

## What the Prop Is NOT

- Not a puzzle in itself (it requires the feeder answers).
- Not a spoiler (song listings are real but do not reveal puzzle answers).
- Not necessary for solving individual puzzles (those live on the website).
