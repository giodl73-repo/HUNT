# Set 6 — Name That Band

*"These are just words. Unless you know who they really are."*

---

Vee's last set. Six songs, six artists. But on the playlist card, she did not write the band names. She wrote definitions. Dictionary definitions, one for each artist. As if the names were just words.

They are just words. But they are also something else.

---

## Vee's Definitions

**1.**
The largest of the seven continents. It stretches from the Ural Mountains to the Pacific, from the Arctic to the Indian Ocean. Home to more than half the world's population.

**2.**
A person or thing that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand. From the Latin *aenigma*, itself from the Greek. Churchill described Russia as one, wrapped in a mystery, inside another.

**3.**
A hollow muscular organ that pumps blood through the circulatory system. In common usage, the seat of emotion and courage. To lose it is to despair.

**4.**
Large birds of prey in the family Accipitridae. Known for broad wingspans, powerful talons, and exceptionally keen eyesight. Several species are national symbols. The bald variety is native to North America.

**5.**
A port city in eastern Massachusetts. Founded in 1630, it was the site of a famous act of colonial defiance involving tea and a harbor. Home to Fenway Park and a marathon that has been run since 1897.

**6.**
The third-largest city in the United States, on the southwestern shore of Lake Michigan. Known for deep-dish pizza, blues music, and architecture. Its nickname references the strong winds off the lake, though some say the name is older than the weather.
