## Extraction Affordances

Track titles where specific letters (by position) could extract useful characters:
- First letters of track titles across an album
- Nth letter of the Nth track
- Last letters of track titles
- Letters at the track number position within the title

These patterns are documented here for puzzle design; specific extraction schemes are in puzzle briefs.
