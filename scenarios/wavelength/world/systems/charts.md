## Chart Position Encoding System (for P4)

The puzzle gives solvers a list of songs. Each song's Billboard Hot 100 peak position corresponds to a letter (A=1 through Z=26). The solver looks up each song's peak position and converts to the corresponding letter.

**Constraint:** Only songs peaking between positions 1-26 are usable. Songs peaking higher (lower number) are more easily verifiable; songs at positions 20+ are harder to verify and rarer for well-known tracks.
