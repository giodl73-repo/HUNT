# Music Theory Reference

Standard Western music theory facts used in puzzles.

---

## Note Names

The 7 natural notes: **A B C D E F G**

These are the only note names that are also single English letters. This is the basis for the music theory puzzle — sequences of notes spell words using the letters A through G.

### Sharps and Flats

| Natural | Sharp | Flat |
|---------|-------|------|
| A | A# | Ab |
| B | B# (enharmonic C) | Bb |
| C | C# | Cb (enharmonic B) |
| D | D# | Db |
| E | E# (enharmonic F) | Eb |
| F | F# | Fb (enharmonic E) |
| G | G# | Gb |

### The Chromatic Scale (ascending from C)

C - C#/Db - D - D#/Eb - E - F - F#/Gb - G - G#/Ab - A - A#/Bb - B

12 semitones per octave.

## Key Signatures

### Major Keys and Their Sharps/Flats

| Key | Sharps/Flats | Notes in scale |
|-----|-------------|----------------|
| C major | 0 | C D E F G A B |
| G major | 1# (F#) | G A B C D E F# |
| D major | 2# (F#, C#) | D E F# G A B C# |
| A major | 3# (F#, C#, G#) | A B C# D E F# G# |
| E major | 4# (F#, C#, G#, D#) | E F# G# A B C# D# |
| B major | 5# (F#, C#, G#, D#, A#) | B C# D# E F# G# A# |
| F major | 1b (Bb) | F G A Bb C D E |
| Bb major | 2b (Bb, Eb) | Bb C D Eb F G A |
| Eb major | 3b (Bb, Eb, Ab) | Eb F G Ab Bb C D |
| Ab major | 4b (Bb, Eb, Ab, Db) | Ab Bb C Db Eb F G |

### Order of Sharps: F C G D A E B
Mnemonic: Father Charles Goes Down And Ends Battle

### Order of Flats: B E A D G C F
Mnemonic: Battle Ends And Down Goes Charles' Father

## Time Signatures

| Signature | Meaning | Common name |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| 4/4 | 4 beats per measure, quarter note = 1 beat | Common time |
| 3/4 | 3 beats per measure | Waltz time |
| 2/4 | 2 beats per measure | March time |
| 6/8 | 6 beats per measure, eighth note = 1 beat | Compound duple |
| 5/4 | 5 beats per measure | Used in "Take Five" by Dave Brubeck |
| 7/8 | 7 beats per measure | Used in "Money" by Pink Floyd |

## Standard Tuning (Guitar)

Low to high: E A D G B E

The open strings of a standard-tuned guitar spell E-A-D-G-B-E. Note that this contains the letters E, A, D, G, B (all valid note-letters).
